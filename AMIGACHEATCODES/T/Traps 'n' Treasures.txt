 CHEAT MODE:

 disk B, type 'SCAMAGIC'. The screen should flash. Now whilst playing

 During play, type 'PKLABAUTERMANNP'. The screen again should flash.
 This will give you one free life. Simply repeat this whenever you
 need another life. You can only have a maximum of 9 lives.
