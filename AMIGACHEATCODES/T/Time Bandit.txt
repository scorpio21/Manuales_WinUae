Egyptian Sector :

 Sentinel       : Find the Ankh on level 2A.  It is needed to read  a
                  scroll in the Guardian, and it is used to  enter 3A
                  on Cheop's.
                  ********There is NO Artifact for this level********
 Cheop's Curse  : To enter level 3A, you need the ANKH from the Sent-
                  inel.  To enter level 4A, it asks for  the  SCEPTOR
                  from the Guardian.  When in Cheop's room,  use  the
                  ankh and the sceptor at the north and south altars.
                  Then use the switches  to bend the light  into  the
                  eye.
                  ********There is an Artifact for this level********
 Gaurdian       : Read the scrolls every level  to find  info  on THE
                  DOOR.  A GOLD SCEPTOR is on level 2B. Indiana Jones
                  map is full of SHIT.
                  ********There is NO Artifact for this level********

Midevil Sector  :

 Arena          : ********There is NO Artifact for this level********
 King's Crown   : Find the crown on level 4D in the deepest  dungeon.
                  Give it to the King when asked.
                  ********There is an Artifact for this level********
 Castle Greymoon: You can reach the Mad Hermit on C  levels by  using
                  the four warpers. The Hermits solutions are as fol-
                  lows: Clubs-Torch, Hearts-Blood, Spades-Grave-Dirt,
                  Diamonds-?. To get the Blood give the wine  to  the
                  sheaphearder.  For grave-dirt  use  rubble.  At the
                  Unicorn answer: No, No, No, You.
