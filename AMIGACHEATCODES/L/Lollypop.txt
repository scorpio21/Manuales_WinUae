LOLLYPOP

     
                       -+-<: keyboard usage :>-+-

        [RETURN] enter extra-area (if standing in front of a door).
          [SPACE] activate photocamera (works like a smartbomb).
                            [P] pause game.
                       [ESC] return to gamemenu.

        check this: if you choose 5 or 7 lives in the options-menu,
                you may only play the first three levels !
             choose 3 lives at start to play the whole game !
        the levelcode-table beyond shows lives saved in password !
