Globdule


 CHEAT MODE:

 Whilst playing, press [Ctrl], left [Shift], left [Alt] and right [Alt]
 simultaneously. Now release all the keys and then press them all again
 untill Globdule begins to flash. This enables these keys during play:

    [C] - Changes score to screen location.        
   [F3] - Meets your quota and opens the exit.
   [F4] - Complete level.
   [F9] - Infinite lives.
  [F10] - Restart current level.
 [Ctrl] - Toggle invincibility.
  [Del] - Fly mode (see keys below).
    [-] - Slows Globdule down in fly mode.
    [+] - Speeds Globdule up in fly mode.

 Pressing the [Del] key on the world/level select screen opens
 up the map so you can jump to any level in any order.
