 TIPS:

 One bonus level itself leads to another bonus level.
 Bonus levels are build from either weaponing or storeroom blocks.
 Codes to bonus levels may only be entered on the specified level.
 Codes to ordinary levels may be entered from any code terminal on
 an earlier level.
