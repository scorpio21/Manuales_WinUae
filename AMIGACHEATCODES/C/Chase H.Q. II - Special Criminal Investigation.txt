Chase HQ II  (S.C.I.)


 Holding keys [H] and [F5] while the game loads makes all the roadside
 obstacles disappear.

 Pause the game and type 'IN A GARDEN IN', now unpause and these keys
 will be enabled:

        [T] - Extra time.
        [W] - Rockets.
        [N] - Next level.
 [1] to [6] - Jump to corresponding level.
