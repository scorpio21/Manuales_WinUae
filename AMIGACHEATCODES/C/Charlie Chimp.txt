Charlie Chimp


 CHEAT MODE:

 Hold down the left mouse button while the game loads and a message will
 appear "CHEAT MODE ACTIVE". You can now use the following keys:

    [1] - Baseball bat.
    [2] - Golf club.
    [3] - Tennis racquet.
    [I] - Invulnaribility.
    [B] - Collect all BONUS letters.
    [E] - Collect all EXTRA letters.
    [L] - Get an extra life.
    [R] - Sets time to 24.
    [T] - Sets time to 99.
 [Help] - Jump to next level.
