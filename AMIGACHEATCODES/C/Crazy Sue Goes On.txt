Crazy Sue Goes On


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the title screen, type 'HIRONZMUSJUMPSHOE'. The border should flash.
 Now use the following keys on the keypad during the game:

 [1] - Stop the timer.
 [2] - Start the timer.
 [3] - High jump.
 [7] - Turn sprite collision off.
 [8] - Turn sprite collision on.
 [.] - Gives you keys.

 Left and right cursor keys control slow motion.
