Flying Shark


 CHEAT MODE:

 Gain a high score and enter the following initials for various
 effects:

 'RHL' - For acid men.
 'JGL' - Full fire power.
 'KDJ' - Infinite lives.
 'RAB' - Immunity.
 'HSC' - To turn the screen black.

 Always hold down [5] on the numeric keypad while typing in
 the last letter of each cheat.
