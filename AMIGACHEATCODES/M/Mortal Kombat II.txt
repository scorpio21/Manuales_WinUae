Mortal Kombat II


 CHEAT MODE:

 The method for getting the cheat menu is very similar to the original
 Mortal Kombat. On the Options screen, type 'ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ'.
 Repeat the process four/five times. Below the done option, Diagnostics
 will appear - where you can watch all of the death moves and toggle
 invincibility on/off etc etc.

 To turn the blood off, type in 'FIONA' on the Main Menu. The screen
 will flash when done correctly.

 Key:

 A = Away
 D = Down
 T = Towards
 U = Up
 F = Fire

 You can only perform Friendship and Babality if there were no Hi Punches
 or Lo Punches in the previous rounds (Uppercuts and Jump Punches are
 still allowed).
