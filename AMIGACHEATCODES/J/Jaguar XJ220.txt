Jaguar XJ220


 CHEAT MODE:

 Before playing the game, select the radio mode on the CD player.
 This will cause the scenery to travel at twice the speed and make
 driving a lot easier.

 Also when your told to start your engine, press the fire button.
 Then when it says "GO" press [P] to pause the game and [P] again to
 unpause the game. You'll finish the race instantly with the fastest
 lap time.
