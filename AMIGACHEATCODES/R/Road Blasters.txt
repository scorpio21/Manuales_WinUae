Road Blasters


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the starting line during play type 'LAVILLASTRANGIATO'.
 You will now be able to press the following keys during play:

 [F] - Extra fuel.
 [G] - End game.
 [S] - Next stage.
 [X] - Spins car.
 [1] - VZ cannons.
 [2] - Cruise missiles.
 [3] - Electro shields.
 [4] - Nitro injectors.
 [0] - Remove weapons.
