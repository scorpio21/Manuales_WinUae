Rubicon


 CHEAT MODE:

 Pause the game by pressing the [Spacebar] and type in 'THEREAPER'.
 This gives you infinite lives and you can press any of the following
 keys during play:

 [F1] to [F7] - Weapon selection.
        [F10] - Recharge weapons.
          [L] - Level skip (only when the game is paused).
