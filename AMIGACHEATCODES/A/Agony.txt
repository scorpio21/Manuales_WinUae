Agony


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the title screen, type in 'FANTASY'. The music will become slightly
 louder and the power light will dim to indicate that you've activated
 the cheat mode. Now during the game you can press:

     [F1] - Sword below owl.
     [F2] - Sword above owl.
     [F3] - Enlarge bullets & 10.000 extra points.
     [F4] - Extra life.
 [Return] - Level skip.
