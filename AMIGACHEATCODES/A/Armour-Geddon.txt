Armour-Geddon


 CHEAT MODE:

 When in the HQ, access the messages screen. Move the arrow so it
 points to the first letter of the message "DAY 1" which is highlighted
 in yellow. Now hold down the [Esc] key and press the left mouse
 button, the message "YOU WOULDN'T LET IT LIE!" will appear.
 This indicates that the cheat mode is now active.
 This makes your craft invincible and the supplies of firepower and
 fuel unlimited. To turn the cheat mode off simply access the message
 screen again and point the arrow to the first letter of the message
 highlighted in yellow. Press and hold down the [Esc] key and click the
 left mouse button, the message "LOOK AT THE SIZE OF THAT SAUSAGE" will
 appear, the cheat mode is now off.


 EDITOR:

 To enter the editor simply move the arrow to the top left corner of
 the secondry title screen and hold down the left [Shift] key and press
 the left mouse button, you will now be greeted by a vertical line.
 This is an object from Armour-Geddon, to view more interesting ones
 try using the arrow keys. Press [F9] and [F10] to zoom in and out of
 the objects and [7] and [8] and both brackets on the numeric keypad to
 rotate them. To exit the object editor simply press [Esc] or any key
 from [F1] to [F6] to access the relevant section, eg, the HQ screen.
