Bubble Dizzy


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the title screen press [E], [A], [S] and [Y] simultaneously to
 allow you to press the [+] and [-] keys whilst playing to skip up
 and down through the levels. Pressing keys [H], [A], [R] and [D] on
 the title screen will turn the cheat off.

 To enter the secret music selection screen press the left and right
 [Shift] keys and the left and right [Alt] keys simultaneously.
