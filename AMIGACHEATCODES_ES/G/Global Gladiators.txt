Global Gladiators


 CHEAT MODE:

 Pause the game and enter the following sequences on the joystick:
 Note that you have to center the joystick after every move.

 U,L,D,R,U,L,F,D,R,F, and F. Now unpause and you'll complete the level.
 To complete the level and go to the bonus game again pause the action
 and enter the following sequence with the joystick:
 U,L,D,R,U,L,F,D,R,F,F,D,R,U,L,F, and F.
