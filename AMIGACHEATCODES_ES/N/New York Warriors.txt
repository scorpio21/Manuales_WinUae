New York Warriors


 CHEAT MODE:

 During play type in 'WAISTVICE'. You can now press [F1] to [F8]
 to Jump to the corresponding level. It also enables these keys:

   [T] - Activate flame thower.
   [G] - Activate grenade launcher.
   [H] - Activate homing missiles.
   [F] - Activate spread fire.
   [S] - Activate normal missiles.
  [F9] - Add guys.
 [F10] - Toggle invincibility.
