-> cheat #1 <-
 Press fire before starting and then a quick diagonal left-up, this will let
 you begin at day 18.
-> cheat #2 <-
 Type 'ALF' into the hi-score table for infinite demerits.
-> cheat #3 <-
 If you press the fire button and push forward on your joystick while the 
 music is playing, you can start playing the game on any of the 16 levels.
