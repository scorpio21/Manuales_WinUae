Charlie Chimp II


 CHEAT MODE:

 Just press the left mouse button on the title screen.
 Now during play use the following keys:

    [B] - Bonus level.
    [E] - Extra level.
    [1] - Gives charlie a baseball bat.
    [2] - Gives charlie a hockey stick.
    [3] - Gives charlie a tennis raquet.
 [Help] - Skip levels.
