Watch the opening scenes... pretty nice, eh?

Terminology:
Get/Put : Use the closed hand
Use     : Use the pointing hand
Look    : Use the eye
Examine : Use the magnifying lense

Notes:
Talk to everyone... this game uses a hypertext like
interface. Always scan the 'conversation bubble' for
words which may lead to other questions than those
displayed.

Watch the actions of other people. Usually their actions
are directly related to items which may be of some use.

Use the eye icon often. As you 'look' at items the icons
bar will indicate what further actions may be taken on
the item.
