                             BUMP N BURN CHEAT
                             -----------------

 On the options page select Exit and wait for the picture of the flag
 to appear. Now type 'ZXR750R' very quickly and the screen will flash.
 Now you'll find that after your first race you'll be awarded 9999.
 Also follow the above procedure but type 'HOUSEY' instead and after
 a bit of disk accessing you'll be treated to some house music.
