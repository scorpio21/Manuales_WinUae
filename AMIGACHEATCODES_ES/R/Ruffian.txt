Cheats:

Wait for the main menu to appear and select OPTIONS.  Select the top
option ENTER PASSWORD and type in any of the following codes for
various effects.  You can type in any number of codes one after the
other to activate as many cheats as you wish.

MANYLIFE  Infinite lives
TIMELESS  Infinite time
FIREMORE  Infinite bullets
CENSORED  Drop trousers and moon at you when idle
SPACEBAR  Activate mega shooting with space bar
CHEATOFF  Turn off all cheats
CANTKILL  Invulnerability

The CENSORED cheat takes a few seconds to see. Enter the cheat and 
start the game. Leave Ruffian idle and he will start to pull faces, 
tap his toes and eventually he will turn around, drop his trousers and 
moon at you!
