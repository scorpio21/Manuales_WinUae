Magic Boy


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the options page hold down the first letter of the level you want
 to jump to and a number between [1] and [2] for what part you wish to
 start playing. Now while pressing these keys hit the fire button to
 begin playing on that level. Here follows a list of all the levels:

 [S] - Sand Land.
 [F] - Future Zone.
 [P] - Plastic Place.
 [W] - Wet World.
