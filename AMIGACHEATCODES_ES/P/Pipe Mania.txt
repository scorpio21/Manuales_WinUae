 PLAYING TIPS:

 For a 4000 point bonus, you need to get the flooze to cross itself five
 times. But remember not to get too cocky, and wait until the end of the
 pipe to do this. There is also a secret bonus of 50,000 points if you
 completely fill the screen with pipes.
