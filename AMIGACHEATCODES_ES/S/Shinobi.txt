 'LARSXVII' - For unlimited credits.
 'LARSXVII' - While holding down left mouse button to enter color mode -
             (this is controlled with numeric pad).

 Also pressing [Q] while the game is paused lets you enter music mode.

 Lastly when the Shinobi logo appears on the title screen hold down the
