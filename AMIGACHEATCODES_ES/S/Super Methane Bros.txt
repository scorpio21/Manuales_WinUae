 CHEAT MODE:

 Enter any of the following with the joystick when the game is paused.

   'NEXT' - Skips level (once only).
  'SPEED' - Makes game faster.
  'MANIC' - Hard setting.
  'CHEAT' - Message.
  'CODES' - Message.
 'DELVIN' - Message.
  'LLOYD' - Message.
