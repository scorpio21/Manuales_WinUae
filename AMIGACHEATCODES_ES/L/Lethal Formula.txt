                   LETHAL FORMULA
                   --------------
       Amiga Graphic Adventure on SynTax Disk 811
            Solution by: Philip Richmond

 TAKE screwdriver, lockpick and laser cutter.
 USE laser cutter on suitcase.
 Walk up stairs then..
 USE electrokey on slot.
 Go through door then..
 OPERATE keyboard.
 You are asked for a code number. Enter 2678.
 (This is a hack of the current Galactic date)
 You now have access to door on second level.
 TAKE fire extinguisher.
 Go through door next to it..
 OPERATE yellow button.
 OPERATE red button.
 OPERATE blue button.

 This should deactivate the laser in ceiling.
 (You can check by operating fire extinguisher)
 Go east
 USE screwdriver on panel
 TAKE fuses
 USE fuse on fuse socket (replace 3rd fuse)
 This should light up second keyboard downstairs..
 TAKE breath filter (from medical cabinet)
 USE blank security card on slot.
 OPERATE keyboard.
 OPERATE slot (to get now validated card)
 Go back up, west and west then up stairs
 USE validated card in slot.
 Go through door..
 OPERATE breath filter (quickly!)
 Go east..
 USE lockpick on safe.

 You are then discovered by Mr. Tough who will
 stun you and capture you...
 You awake, strapped to a chair in the medical lab..
 and injected with Substance H..

 Go east..
 TAKE gun
 EXAMINE cop (to get paycard, notebook and disguise)
 Go back to medical lab..
 Enter lift.. and go to Canteen...
 OPERATE keyboard (on drinks machine) Enter number 157
 USE paycard on slot (you now have neutron explosive)
 OPERATE keyboard (on drinks machine) Enter number 111
 USE paycard on slot (you now have a drink)
 OPERATE drink (you now have an empty cup)
 TAKE cloth
 Go east, enter lift, go to medical lab..
 USE cup on tap
 OPERATE panel
 USE cloth on cup (to take liquid nitrogen)
 Go west, enter lift, go to Level 3
 USE cup on hatch
 OPERATE hatch
 OPERATE hatch (again to go down)
 USE lockpick on locker
 TAKE detonator (from locker)
 USE neutron explosive on detonator
 OPERATE ladder
 Go east, enter lift, go to medical lab..
 OPERATE bomb
 Go in lift and select Emergency Exit
 OPERATE disguise
 Go west through door...
 SHOOT Mr. Tough (quick before he escapes)

 That's it....

 There now follows an imaginative end sequence
 and congratulations.
