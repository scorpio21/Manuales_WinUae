CHEAT CODE :


- SOME CLUES :

- Endless life : type SHIFT + SHIFT + "+" when the Silmarils logo is displayed at the beginning of the game (loading phase).

- If you want to go directly to a village : type SHIFT + SHIFT + "V" when you start the driving arcade.

- If you want to go directly to the Maya's city or to Merida : type SHIFT + SHIFT + "C" when you start the driving arcade.

SOLUTION :



- MERIDA :
Go to the shop and buy : lamp, first aid kit, lever, cigarettes, water bottle, fruit, meat. Return to the jeep.


- JEEP TO UXMAL :
Take the first track to the left.


- FIRST VILLAGE :
Exchange 2 vases and "disk of the winds" against the cigarettes, the water bottle and the meat.


- JEEP TO MERIDA :
Straight on.


- MERIDA :
Go to the shop, sell the 2 vases and buy phonograph, umbrella, engine oil, gasoline and meat. Return to the jeep.


- JEEP TO UXMAL : 
Take the following bifurcations : right, right, right.


- UXMAL : 
(save the game : press key F10) go to the right and go to the back. Enter into the temple on the right, first door : take the statuette. Go to the temple on the left and first door : look at the fresco, second door : take the small plate and return to the jeep. If you have been bitten by the snakes, use the syringes of the first aid kit.


- JEEP TO TIKAL :
Take the following bifurcations : right, left.


- SECOND VILLAGE :
Exchange the statuette and the part of fetish of clay against the umbrella and the meat.


- JEEP TO TIKAL : 
Straight on.


- TIKAL : 
(save the game : press key F10) go to the left and go to the back of the first temple. Identify the 2 plates which are on the door (to watch : press key F8). Return to the jeep and go to the right and enter into the third temple and go to the back. Take the small plate and lift the slab (press key F6), push once the panel corresponding to the first plate.

Return to the jeep and go to the temple on the back : take the statuette, lift the slab (press key F6), push the panel corresponding to the second plate and return to the temple on the left. Enter into the temple and take the part of fetish. Return to the jeep. If you have been stung by the darts, use the bandages and the syringes of the first aid kit. In order to avoid the darts, walk diagonally.


- JEEP TO COPAN :
Take the following bifurcations : left, left.


- THIRD VILLAGE : 
Exchange the small plate against the phonograph.


- JEEP TO COPAN :
Take the following bifurcations : left, right.


- COPAN :
(save the game : press key F10) go to the left, take the passage through the jungle (on the back and the left of the scene). Go twice to the left and avoid the carnivorous plants. Place yourself near the stele shaped like an obelisk. Lift the stele (press key F6) and take the statuette. Return to the jeep. If you have been bitten by the plants, use the bandages. Go to the right until you meet the temple and enter into it by the left door.


Take the vases and the fruits, then return to the central door and watch the two frescoes (press key F8). Go to the right door and place yourself on the most empty slab. If you link properly the three small plates and if you translate the hieroglyphs by using the "disk of the winds" then you get directions to the North, to the East and to the South and you go across the three rooms with the maces. You have to read the plates line by line and if you make a mistake you will receive a mace on your head. In this case, you will have to restart the path from the beginning and make use of the bandages. Once you have gone across the three rooms, a door opens. Enter and take the part of fetish. In order to reopen the door, you have to put back on the balance the part of fetish of clay. Then, go out and go across again the rooms by following the same path. Return to the jeep.


- JEEP TO TIKAL :
Take the following bifurcations : left, right, right, right.


- JEEP TO UXMAL : 
Take the following bifurcations : left, left.


- UXMAL :
(save the game : press key F10). Go to the sorcerer in the central temple. Put down the four statuettes in conformity to the fresco. Go out and go to the right temple, enter by the second door and take the last part of festish. Return to the jeep.


- JEEP TO MERIDA :
Take the following bifurcations : right, right, left.


- MERIDA : 
Go to the shop, sell the vases and the "disk of the winds" and buy gasoline (3 or 4 cans) and oil (2 cans)
(save the game).


- JEEP TO UXMAL :
Take the following bifurcations : right, right, right.


- JEEP TO TIKAL :
Take the following bifurcations : right, right.


- JEEP TO COPAN :
Take the following bifurcations : left, right, left, right.


- JEEP TO CHICHEN ITZA :
Take the following bifurcations : right, left, right, left.


- CHICHEN ITZA :
(save the game) go up the pyramid. If you want to avoid the eagle, you must strike it many times, then it will fly away for a while. If you have been stricken by the eagle, use the bandages. Enter into the pyramid and go down.

Fight against your enemy ("Olrik Karloff") : you must push him to the gap by striking him endlessly (easy if you are on the front and in the right of the scene).

Riddle of the bridges : when you put down the (reconstructed) fetish on a stone, you set in action one of the bridges. Each time you walk on a slab and you come from a bridge, the other bridge set in action. You must put down properly the fetish on the several stones in order to go across all the (four) bridges. The right combination is : stone 1 - stone 2 - stone 4 when you come from the front of the screen to the back. Don't forget to pick up the fetish before going across the bridges. On the other side, put down the fetish on the stone and enter.
