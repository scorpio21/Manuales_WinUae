                             DOUBLE DRAGON 3
                             ===============
THE STORY.
----------
THE LEE BROTHERS FACE THEIR TOUGHEST MISSION TO DATE AS HIRUKO THE ANCIENT
SOOTHSAYER GUIDES THEM TO THE POSSIBLE LOCATION OF THE ELUSIVE ROSETTA STONE.

THIS JOURNEY WILL CALL UPON THEIR GREATEST SKILLS AS THEY FACE TREACHERY AND
EVIL FORCES ON THEIR MISSIONS THROUGH EXOTIC LANDSCAPES. BILLY AND JIMMY WILL
NEED TO WORK TOGETHER TO DISCOVER ONCE AND FOR ALL THE EVIL THAT LIES BEHIND
THE SECRET OF THE STONES.WILL THEIR YEARS OF MEDITATION AND TRAINING FOR THEM
TO SURVIVE THIS PERILOUS JOURNEY?..THE CHOICE IS YOURS.

CONTROLS.
---------
`WITHOUT fire button pressed`
-----------------------------
WALK UP AND LEFT   (INTO SCREEN)   ; PUSH JOYSTICK `TOP LEFT`
WALK UP            (INTO SCREEN)   ; PUSH JOYSTICK UP AND `CENTRE`
WALK UP AND RIGHT  (INTO SCREEN)   ; PUSH JOYSTICK `TOP RIGHT`
WALK LEFT                          ; PUSH JOYSTICK `LEFT`
WALK RIGHT                         ; PUSH JOYSTICK `RIGHT`
WALK LEFT AND DOWN (OUT OF SCREEN) ; PULL JOYSTICK `BOTTOM LEFT`
WALK RIGHT AND DOWN(OUT OF SCREEN) ; PULL JOYSTICK `BOTTOM RIGHT`
WALK DOWN          (OUT OF SCREEN) ; PULL JOYSTICK `DOWN AND CENTRE`

`WITH fire button pressed`
--------------------------
PUSH JOYSTICK `TOP LEFT`    FOR    ; JUMP AND LEFT..IF JOYSTICK PUSHED AND LET
                                     GO - JUMP KICK. KICK IF JOYSTICK HELD IN
                                     POSITION.

PUSH JOYSTICK `TOP RIGHT`   FOR    ; JUMP AND RIGHT.IF JOYSTICK PUSHED AND LET
                                     GO.JUMP IF JOYSTICK HELD IN POSITION.

PUSH JOYSTICK `TOP CENTRE`  FOR    ; JUMP IF JOYSTICK PUSHED UP AND  LET GO.
                                     JUMP KICK IF JOYSTICK HELD IN UP POSITION.

PUSH JOYSTICK `CENTRE LEFT` FOR    ; KICK LEFT

PUSH JOYSTICK `CENTRE RIGHT`FOR    ; KICK RIGHT

PULL JOYSTICK `CENTRE AND BOTTOM`  ; TRICKS (IF YOU HAVE THEM)
                                     AND PICK UP WEAPONS.

JOYSTICK JUST CENTRED       FOR    ; PUNCH
