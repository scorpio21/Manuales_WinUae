THE BOMB LAYER GUIDE
--------------------
                             THE CONTROLS
                             ------------
EACH PLAYER MOVES WITH A JOYSTICK,AND LAYS BOMBS WHEN PRESSING THE FIRE
BUTTON. TO PAUSE THE GAME,PRESS `P`. TO RESUME GAME, PRESS `P` AGAIN.

THE PLAYERS USE THE FOLLOWING CONTROLS
--------------------------------------
ON ATARI ST AND AMIGA. PLAYERS 1 AND 2 USE THE ADAPTOR INCLUDED IN THE
GAME PACKAGE (UNLESS OF COURSE YOURS IS A PIRATE COPY). PLAYERS 3 AND 4 USE
THE JOYSTICK PORTS. PLAYER 5 USES THE KEYBOARD (WITH CURSOR KEYS AND SPACE
BAR,OR WITH 2,4,6,8 AND 5 KEYS)

THE AIM OF THE GAME
-------------------
IN ONE PLAYER MODE;SELECT THE GAME START OPTION, YOU MUST LAY BOMBS IN
ORDER TO DESTROY ALL THE MONSTERS IN THE AREA. BE CAREFUL NOT TO BE BLOWN
UP BY YOUR OWN BOMBS!. TO AVOID THIS,HIDE BEHIND THE WALLS. WHEN ALL THE
MONSTERS HAVE BEEN ELIMINATED, YOU MUST LEAVE THE AREA THROUGH A BLUE
BLINKING DOOR. THE DOOR IS HIDDEN SOMEWHERE IN THE WALLS, YOU WILL HAVE TO
BLOW WALLS UNTIL YOU FIND IT.
WHEN YOU LOSE, A PASSWORD SYSTEM ALLOWS YOU TO RESTART AT THE SAME LEVEL.
JUST SELECT `PASSWORD` IN THE MAIN MENU.

IN 2 TO 5 PLAYERS MODE; SELECT THE BATTLE OPTION. 5 PLAYERS CAN PLAY
TOGETHER ON ATARI ST (DIDNT KNOW ANYONE HAD THEM ANYMORE HEHEHE) AMIGA, AND
PC (ITS ON 500 DISKS ON THE PC) WHEN SEVERAL PLAYERS BATTLE TOGETHER, THE
WINNER IS THE LAST SURVIVOR, YOU CAN USE ALL KINDS OF TRICKS TO WIN THE
GAME.

SYMBOLS
-------
FLAME ICON...........FLAME; MAKES THE EXPLOSION BIGGER

BOMB ICON............BOMB ; YOU COLLECT A BOMB

ROLLER SKATES ICON...ROLLERSKATE; THE GAME SPEED IS FASTER

REMOTE CONTROL ICON..HEART; BOMBS DO NOT EXPLODE AUTOMATICALLY, BUT ONLY
WHEN YOU PRESS ON THE SPACE BAR.

BOMB ICON............PAINTED BOMB; YOU CAN PASS THROUGH BOMBS

FACE ICON............CHARACTER; THIS ICON GIVES YOU A BONUS LIFE

BRICK ICON...........PAINTED WALL; YOU CAN PASS THROUGH WALLS

JACKET ICON..........JACKET; THE EXPLOSIONS HAVE NO EFFECT ON YOU

IN MULTI PLAYER MODE
--------------------
FLAME ICON...........FLAME; MAKES THE EXPLOSION BIGGER

BOMB ICON............BOMB; YOU COLLECT A BOMB

SKULL: THIS ICON ONLY APPEARS IN MULTI PLAYER MODE, IF THE `SKULL` MODE HAS
BEEN SELECTED, IT MAY HAVE A VARIOUS RANGE OF EFFECTS, AS FOLLOWS...

YOU CANNOT LAY BOMBS FOR A CERTAIN AMOUNT OF TIME

YOU DROP THEM AS SOON AS YOU COLLECT THEM

YOU MOVE FASTER OR SLOWER

OR THE EXPLOSIONS ARE VERY SMALL

AS SOON AS THE PLAYER IS AFFECTED BY THIS, HE STARTS BLINKING. IF HE
TOUCHES ANOTHER PLAYER, THE PLAYER WILL BE CONTAMINATED, THE EFFECT STOPS
WHEN THE PLAYER STOPS BLINKING.

COMPLEMENTARY NOTES
-------------------
IF YOUR COMPUTER IS CONNECTED TO A TV THROUGH A TV MODULATOR, YOU CAN 
SWITCH TO 50HZ DISPLAY BY PRESSING BOTH MOUSE BUTTONS WHILE THE PROGRAM IS
LOADING. THE DEFAULT MODE IS 60HZ. YOU CAN ACTIVATE OR DEACTIVATE THE SOUND
FILTER DURING THE GAME. JUST PRESS `S` IF YOUR COMPUTER IS CONNECTED TO A
HIFI SYSTEM, USE THIS OPTION FOR A BETTER SOUND QUALITY.
