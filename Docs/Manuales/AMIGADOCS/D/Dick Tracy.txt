-->  (CURSOR).....WALK RIGHT
<--  (CURSOR).....WALK LEFT
DOWN (CURSOR).....GET DOWN

SPACE BAR+

--> (CURSOR)..................SHOOT HORIZONTALLY RIGHT
--> (CURSOR) AND UP (CURSOR)..SHOOT DIAGONALLY RIGHT
UP  (CURSOR)..................SHOOT UPWARDS
<-- (CURSOR) AND UP (CURSOR)..SHOOT DIAGONALLY LEFT
<-- (CURSOR)..................SHOOT HORIZONTALLY LEFT
PRESS RETURN..................TO CHANGE WEAPON

TO HAVE A FIST FIGHT..USING KEYBOARD

--> (CURSOR).....PUNCH RIGHT
<-- (CURSOR).....PUNCH LEFT

WHEN DICK TRACEY IS UP ON THE ROOF TOPS..

--> (CURSOR) AND UP (CURSOR)..JUMP RIGHT
<-- (CURSOR) AND UP (CURSOR)..JUMP LEFT


CONTROL PANEL FOR THE GAME...

FAR LEFT OF THE PANEL IS AMOUNT OF BULLETS LEFT IN TRACEY`S PISTOL
NEXT TO THIS(2ND LEFT) IS THE WEAPON SELECTED
NEXT TO THIS(3RD LEFT)IS AMOUNT OF BULLETS LEFT IN TRACEY`S MACHINE GUN
NEXT TO THIS(2ND RIGHT)IS THE ENERGY LEVEL OF DICK TRACEY
BELOW THIS(2ND RIGHT) IS THE HIGH SCORE
AND FAR RIGHT IS - WHEN A GANSTERS HEAD IS DISPLAYED IN PLACE OF DICK
TRACEY`S,THIS TELL YOU THAT YOU ARE GOING TO ENCOUNTER THIS PERSON IN THE NEXT
FRAME.

MOVEMENTS USING THE `JOYSTICK`

WITH-OUT THE `fire button being pressed`PUSHING J/STICK `LEFT` MOVE DT LEFT
WITH-OUT THE `fire button being pressed`PUSHING J/STICK `RIGHT` MOVES DT RIGHT
WITH-OUT THE `fire button being pressed`PUSHING DOWN MAKES DT STOOPD/DUCK

WITH THE `fire button pressed` PUSHING J/STICK TOP LEFT MAKES DT FIRE TOP LEFT
WITH THE `fire button pressed` PUSHING J/STICK TOP RIGHT MAKES DT FIRE T/RIGHT
