THE GOLDEN PYRAMID

Backups
-------
This program is not copy protected.  Micro Entertainment grants to the
purchaser of this product the right to make duplicates for PERSONAL BACKUP
ONLY.  Before using the GOLDEN PYRAMID program, it is important to make a
backup copy of the diskette, and store the master in a safe place.  Refer
to the Amiga User's Guide for DiskCopy instructions.

Getting Started
---------------
Please make sure that the copy of kickstart you put into machine when you
first power it up is version 1.2.  Wait for the Workbench 1.2 prompt, eject
Kickstart and insert the GOLDEN PYRAMID diskette in the internal drive.
When the Workbench screen appears, double-click the GOLDEN PYRAMID disk
icon with the left mouse-button, next double-click the PYRAMID icon to
start the program.

Game Controls:
--------------
All  game  controls  are  activiated  by pointing the mouse cursor over the
required  Gadget,  and  clicking  the  left  mouse  button,  Throughout the
instructions, the above action will be referred to as CLICK.  For those who
prefer  using the keyboard, there are keyboard equivalents for all Gadgets,
except the selection of a Stone from the GOLDEN PYRAMID.

Current Player Options:
-----------------------
Select A Consonant - CLICK the Money Bar Gadget.  When it stops on a dollar
 		     value,  CLICK  your  letter.   If you land on a hidden
	             Pyramid,  CLICK  one  of  the  Stones  in  the  GOLDEN
                     PYRAMID.  Good Luck!

Select  A  Vowel  -  CLICK  the  Vowel  Gadget.   After the vowel amount is
	             deducted from your current Puzzle Total, you can CLICK
                     a vowel.

Solve  the  Puzzle  - CLICK the Solve Gadget; then CLICK the correct letter
	              for  each  highlighted  position in the Puzzle. 
                      Watch Your Spelling!

Ask  For  Help!   -  CLICK the Help!  Gadget; then listen to your Game Show
                     Host while he explains you current options.

End the Game - CLICK the Exit Gadget.  A requester shows up asking you for
	       a  New  Game.   The  choices are Yes, No, or Return to
               Current Game.

Keyboard Equivalents:
---------------------
F1 - Ask For Help
F2 - Not Used
F3 - End the Game
F4 - Solve the Puzzle
F5 - Not Used
F6 - Not Used
F7 - Select a Vowel
F8 - Not Used
F9 - Spin the Money Bar
