  Amiga/Atari ST
  --------------

  Insert Disk 1 into your machine and switch on the computer.  The
  game will load automatically.

  Controls
  --------

  MOVEMENT     ST/AMIGA

  Controller   JOYSTICK
  Left         LEFT
  Right        RIGHT
  Up           DOWN
  Down         UP
  Bullets      HOLD DOWN ON FIRE
  Missiles     QUICK PRESS ON FIRE
  After burner HELP
  Speed up/    0-9 ON KEYPAD
  Slow down
  Pause        P

  Scenario
  --------

  In the hot seat of an experimental super plane you are the ace pilot
  ahead of you lies 36 action packed suicide missions.  Your weaponary
  is highly impressive.  Twin rapid fire lazers, and an awesome missile 
  guidance system gives you the technological edge needed for your 
  missions.  In your hands you have such advanced fire power and controls
  that are well ahead of your opposition.

  G-LOC delivers the pulse pounding excitement you'll just want to keep
  coming back for!
