FANTASY WORLD DIZZY
-------------------

DIZZYS CARTOON ADVENTURE IN FANTASY WORLD -EXPLORE DRAGONS LAIRS,WIZARDS CLOUD
CASTLE,THE EVIL KINGS PALACE AND DUNGEONS,MYSTERIOUS NEW WORLDS,VOLCANOES,CLIMB
THE MAGIC BEANSTALK AND MORE...THE INCREDIBLE SEQUEL TO `TREASURE ISLAND DIZZY`

DIZZY AND DAISY STROLLED THROUGH THE ENCHANTED FOREST WITHOUT A CARE IN THE
WORLD...SUDDENLY THE EVIL KINGS TROLLS SEIZED POOR DIASY!.SHE WAS SENT TO
WIZARD WEIRDS TALLEST TOWER,WHILE DIZZY WAS DRAGGED AWAY AND THROWN INTO THE
DEEPEST DARKEST DUNGEON IN THE BOWELS OF FANTASY WORLD.DIZZY WAS
FRIGHTENED!,WHO KNEW WHAT FAYTE AWAITED HIM...AND HE STILL HAD`NT DONE THIS
WEEKS HOMEWORK!!.BUT THEN HE REMEMBERED HE HAD A FRESH GREEN APPLE THAT HE HAD
PLANNED TO GIVE HIS TEACHER TO ESCAPE DETENTION,AND HE CHEERED UP. AND THERE
WAS SOME BREAD AND WATER ON THE TABLE!. A CUNNING PLAN BREWED IN HIS MIND.

BUT DIZZY CAN`T DO EVERYTHING ON HIS OWN...YOU MUST HELP HIM.

JOYSTICK.      ACTION
---------      ------
FIRE           START GAME
LEFT           MOVE LEFT
RIGHT          MOVE RIGHT
UP             JUMP UP
FIRE           PICK UP/DROP/USE

USE UP/DOWN TO SELECT AN OBJECT `DIZZY` IS CARRYING.

PRESS THE FOLLOWING KEYS DURING A GAME.
---------------------------------------
KEYS           ACTION
----           ------
L              TO ALTERNATE BETWEEN MUSIC OR SOUND FX(THE DISC WILL LOAD THIS)
CTRL           TO PAUSE THE GAME
SPACE          TO DISPLAY THE MAP (WHEN YOU GET IT)
ESC            TO EXIT THE GAME.

THE OBJECT IS TO RESCUE DAISY,BUT THERE IS A FURTHER MISSION ONCE YOU HAVE
COMPLETED THIS. YOU HAVE THREE LIVES. BE CAREFUL AROUND WATER , EVERYONE KNOWS
EGGS BREATHE AIR!!..NOTE:THE WILDLIFE CAN BE VERY DANGEROUS!!
