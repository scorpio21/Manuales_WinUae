COMMANDS:
---------
Use joystick to move your character

In game you can change character pressing following cursor keys:

MECH    : press LEFT CURSOR
KAHEA   : press UP CUSOR
UNINKIN : press RIGHT CURSOR

To take item: just moves your character on it
(Token item appears in one of 3 box in right top corner of the screen)

To change selected item: press  LEFT ALT or LEFT AMIGA keys

To use selected item: press SPACE BAR key

To drop selected item: press LEFT SHIFT key

To select magic powers: press F1 to F5
(KAHEA start game with only one magic power)

To pause game: press P key

To unpause: game press O key

To suicide actual character: press HELP key


SKILLS OF THE CHARACTERS
------------------------

KAHEA:
He is able to fly and jump when he have wings closed

To open wings  : press FIRE & UP (JOYSTICK)
To close wings : press FIRE & BACK DOWN

He is quick and able to endure when he have closed wings

First MAGIC POWER: shockwave

He is only character who are able to learn and use others magic power


MECH:
He is able to run, crouch and climb on wall

For big jump: go to DOWN and quickly go to UP

He can't fly but he is able to endure and is able to go trough narrow ways

MAGIC POWER: fireball


UNINKIN:
She is able to fly and trasform himself in flower

Flower trasformation: press FIRE and DOWN (2 seconds)
To throw poison (only flower); press FIRE when enemy walks before flower
To take original form: press FIRE and UP

She is a good fighter and she is invulnerable in flower transformation.
Flower poison is powerfull

MAGIC POWER: Blow of madness


ENERGY AND LIVE
---------------
You can see 2 gauges on the top of the screen:
- top gauge is live level of actual character
- second gauge is energy level of actual character

To increase energy and live levels you must found crytal, soul of killed enemies,
potions and foods.

KAHEA is able to rest when he have wings closed
MEGH is able to rest when he don't move
UNINKIN can't rest to increase live and energy


PROGRESSION IN GAME
-------------------
To finish level you must guide all characters in the exit.
You can continue if MEGH and/or UNINKIN is dead but you lose game if KAHEA dies.

Fantasy Flyer isn't only an action game, you have some enigmas to solve.
Indeed you must found magic power and items to progress.

SAVEGAME CODE:
Between 2 levels, a code is displayed on the screen. Type it in code menu to
restart game at this position.
