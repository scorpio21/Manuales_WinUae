FAST FOOD
---------

FAST FOOD - FINGER LICKIN FUN!,EAT THE PEPPERONI PIZZA,SPICY CHICKEN AND BIG
JUICY BURGERS,THEN CATCH DESERT - A MILKSHAKE,KETCHUP AND RELISH!.YUK.BUT WATCH
OUT FOR BONZO,WIZZA AND PIPPA COS THEY ARE AFTER YOU!!.

CONTROLS.
---------
KEYS       JOYSTICK       ACTION
----       --------       ------
Z          LEFT           WALK LEFT
X          RIGHT          WALK RIGHT
K          UP             WALK UP
M          DOWN           WALK DOWN? (SEZ WALK RIGHT ON THE INSTRUCTIONS?)

PRESS P   TO PAUSE
PRESS ESC TO QUIT.

TO PLAY
-------
MOVE LEFT OR RIGHT TO SELECT YOUR WORLD TO EXPLORE THEN MOVE UP TO ENTER THE
WORLD.CHOOSE FROM `PARK` (EASY),`SUPERMARKET` (MEDIUM) OR `TOYTOWN` (HARD).
ENTRY TO THE 4TH AND FINAL WORLD IS ONLY BY COMPLETING THE HARD WORLD.

WHAT YOU`LL FIND IN THE MAZES
-----------------------------
BURGERS.....
THESE ARE JUST BLIND AND THEY TRAVEL AROUND NOT KNOWING WHERE DIZZY IS.

CHICKENS...
JUST DONT WANT TO BE EATEN.THEY ARE FAST AND WILL RUN AWAY FROM YOU.

PIZZA`S....
DONT MIND BEING EATEN..THATS LIFE WHEN YOUR A PIZZA..THEY ARE EASY TO CATCH

MILKSHAKES...
ARE THE EASIEST OF THEM ALL.THEY WANT TO BE DRUNK..THEY ACTUALLY RUN TO YOU!

sometime mysterious things fall into the maze......

MAGIC BOOTS...
WHICH DOUBLE DIZZYS SPEED.YOU`LL HAVE TO THINK FAST,BUT YOU OUTRUN EVERYTHING

RELISH... IN A GREEN BOTTLE..(SLOWS DOWN MONSTERS)

MUSTARD.. IN AN ORANGE BOTTLE(FREEZES THE MONSTERS)

KETCHUP.. IN A RED BOTTLE WAVE BYE BYE TO THOSE HORRIBLE MONSTERS!

ALSO LOOK FOR SHIELDS WHICH LET DIZZY EAT THE MONSTERS,TOKENS TO MAKE YOU
INVISIBLE AND TOKENS TO SLOW DOWN OR SPEED UP THE GAME.
