THE STORY:

ONLY A 200IB MASS OF ANGRY MUSCLE STANDS BETWEEN YOU AND THE TITLE YOU`VE BEEN
TRAINING FOR ALL YOUR LIFE. LIMBERING UP IN THE OTHER CORNER, AND YOUR READY TO
INLICT SOME SERIOUS DAMAGE,COULD BE ANY 1 OF 10 BOXERS. YOU PUCH,DUCK AND
WEAVE, WITH THE REFEREE WAITING LIKE A VULTURE TO SEE YOU FLOORED.
WILL YOU WEAR THE CHAMPIONSHIP BELT OR WILL THEY BE SHOVELLING YOUR REMAINS
BACK IN A CARDBOARD BOX?

HOW TO PLAY:

FIRST OF ALL YOU CAN ADJUST THE GAME TO YOUR OWN REQUIREMENTS,YOUR OPTIONS ARE:

MINUTES PER ROUND        1     3     5     9

NO OF ROUNDS PER MATCH   1     3     5     0

ORDER OF OPPONENTS     RANDOM           FIXED 1-9

NO OF PLAYERS          1 PLAYER         2 PLAYERS

WHEN PLAYING YOU MUST SELECT 1 BOXER FROM THE 10 AVAILABLE. ALL BOXERS HAVE A
RATING FROM 1-10, 1 BEING THE WORST AND 10 BEING THE BEST, THE BOXERS NAMES AND
RATING ARE:

BOXER                RATING     BOXER        RATING

DYNAMITE JOE           1        KRUSHA KEV     6
KIM NANG               2        COOL SHAUN     7
KING JASON             3        KILLER KEN     8
FERNADO GOMEZ          4        KID STEVE      9
DETROIT KID            5        DEMON DAVE    10

WHEN YOU SELECT A BOXER HIS RATING IS WIPED CLEAN AND ITS UP TO YOU TO MAKE HIM
THE WORLDS BEST.

SPECTATOR MODE:

IN SPECTATOR MODE THE COMPUTER WILL PLAY AGAINST ITSELF, HERE YOU CAN SIT BACK
AND WATCH THE ACTION.

PRACTISE MODE:

GET USED TO THE CONTROLS AND PRACTISE AGAINST THE BEST IN THE WORLD IN
PREPARATION FOR YOUR ATTEMPT AT THE TITLE.

LEAGUE PLAY:

SELECT YOUR BOXER AND TRY TO BASH YOUR WAY THROUGH THE 9 OPPONENTS WHO STAND
BETWEEN YOU AND THE WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE.

LEAGUE PLAY TAKES PLACE OVER 9 WEEKS WITH 9 MATCHES IN EACH WEEK. YOU WILL TAKE
PART IN ONE PER WEEK. EVERY WEEK BOXERS WILL GAIN POINTS FOR WINNING,LOSING OR
DRAWING MATCHES. POINTS ARE AWARDED AS FOLLOWS:

WIN......3 POINTS
DRAW.....1 POINT
LOSE.....0 POINT

THE WINNER IS THE PLAYER WHO HAS THE MOST POINTS AT THE END OF THE 9 WEEK
PERIOD.
