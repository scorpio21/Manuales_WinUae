" THE FIRST POST "
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
ON THE TITLE SCREEN ::::::   WAGGLE THE JOYSTICK FROM " LEFT " TO " RIGHT "
TO MAKE YOUR HORSE GO FASTER. IF YOU MANAGE TO WIN THE RACE THEN YOU WILL
EARN EXTRA CREDITS IN THE GAME AND ALSO A HIGHER " NUDGEPOT " AND 
"CASHPOT ". WHEN YOU HAVE HAD ENOUGH OF PISSING ABOUT ON THIS SCREEN THEN
JUST WACK THE LEFT MOUSE BUTTON TO PLAY THE GAME !



"  1ST HURDLE  "
   ~~~~~~~~~~
THE MAIN GAME :::::  USE THE MOUSE TO CONTROL ALL GAME ACTIONS. THE LEFT
MOUSE IS USED SPECIFICALLY FOR "HOLD", "START"/"GAMBLE" AND "CANCEL"/
"COLLECT".

CLICKING WITH THE RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON WILL AT ANY TIME MOVE YOU TO THE LIST
SCREENS. THESE RUN THROUGH ALL THE WINNING OPTIONS. CLICKING AGAIN WITH THE
MOUSE REVERTS BACK TO THE GAME.



"  2ND HURDLE "
   ~~~~~~~~~~
THE FIRST HIDDEN GAME " PAINTER "
                        ~~~~~~~

WHEN YOU ARE ON THE MAIN PLAYING SCREEN... JUST CLICK THE RED " MI " SYMBOL
ON THE RACETRACK AT THE TOP OF THE SCREEN. THIS WILL ENTER YOU INTO THE 1ST
HIDDEN SUBGAME "PAINTER" ( BASED ON THE GAME AMIDAR ).

1ST HIDDEN SUBGAME "PAINTER"
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
THE PAINTER SUB-GAME HAS ONSCREEN INSTRUCTIONS BUT IT IS BASICALLY YOU RUN
AROUND THE SCREEN AVOIDING ALIENS AND PAINT LINES TO FORM BOXES WHICH WILL
DESTROY THE ALIENS. YOU OF COURSE NEED TO AVOID 'EM TO WIN.
TO START THE GAME YOU USE THE LEFT MOUSE BUTTON OR THE JOYSTICK FIRE BUTTON

THE CONTROL OF THE PLAYER IN PAINTER IS BY JOYSTICK ONLY

TO EXIT THE GAME YOU JUST PRESS THE RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON..AND PRESTO BACK TO
THE MAIN GAME SCREEN. 

" 3RD HURDLE "
  ~~~~~~~~~~

THE SECOND HIDDEN PART "DEMO" AND "2ND HIDDEN GAME"
                        ~~~~       ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

WHILST YOU ARE VIEWING THE WINNING LINES PAGES ..JUST HIT THE JOYSTICK FIRE
BUTTON AND YOU WILL BE TRANSFERRED INTO THE "MENTAL IMAGES" DEMO. WHILST IN 
THE DEMO HIT THE FIRE BUTTON ON THE JOYSTICK AGAIN AND THIS ACCESSES THE
2ND HIDDEN GAME.
