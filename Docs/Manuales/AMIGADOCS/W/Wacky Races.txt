THE AIM OF THE GAME.
-------------------
THE AIM OF THE GAME IS TO GET TO THE WINNING LINE BEFORE THE OTHER RACES.BEING
THE CHEAT THAT YOU ARE,YOU USE ALL OF THE SNEAKY AND DESPICABLE MEANS AT YOUR
DISPOSAL TO ENSURE THAT THE OTHER RACERS DO NOT REACH THE LINE BEFORE YOU.
ASSISTED BY MUTTLEY.YOUR TASK IS TO GET FAR ENOUGH IN FRONT OF THE OTHER RACERS
TO GIVE YOU TIME TO FIND THE REQUIRED PARTS TO BUILD A TRAP FOR THE FOLLOWING
CAR.
EACH SECTION COVERS A RACE FROM ONE CITY TO ANOTHER ACROSS DIFFERENT AREAS. YOU
AND THE MEAN MACHINE IN A BETTER POSITION IN EACH RACE SECTION TO BE ABLE TO
PROGRESS ONTO THE TRAP LYING SECTION BETWEEN EACH ONE.

HINTS AND TIPS.
--------------
* WATCH OTHER CARS TO LEARN THE FASTEST ROUTE IN EACH COURSE
* COLLECT BLUE SPHERES TO BOOST YOUR ENERGY LEVEL
* COLLECT HEARTS TO INCREASE YOUR NUMBER OF LIVES
* IN THE TRAP LAYING SECTION USE THE ENEMIES TO GET YOU YOUR PIECE OF TRAP TO
  MUTTLEY.
* ARROWS POINT TO TELL YOU WHETHER TO DROP OR JUMP
