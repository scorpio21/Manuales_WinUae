                       Warlock the Avenger Quick Docs
                                (The Druid)


F1 - Keys (Face door while pressing button to unlock them)
F2 - Temporarily freeze evil beings (Lasts about 3-4 seconds)
F3 - Invokes Golem Statue (That is, if you have one)
F4 - Calls Chaos into the realm to kill evil beings (Providing you have the
       power... Lasts about 3-4 seconds... Use sparingly...)
F6 - Toggles between your available powers (Water, Fire, and Electricity)
F10 - Toggles Golem between manual or wait (Default is wait)

P - Pause the game (with music)

Note: When Golem is invoked, another player can control him with a
second joystick. (Press F10 for manual movement)

Invoking Golem Statue: Make sure you have enough room to invoke the
statue. This is determined by the direction you face. You cant cast it
facing a wall or a tree.

Pentagrams: Move around pentagram to replenish health. The Pentagrams are
scattered through out the levels of the realm.

Hint: At first treasure chest on level 1, take the KEY!
