                           AUNT ARCTIC ADVENTURE

INSTRUCTION

Insert the Program Disk at the Workbench prompt.
When the introductory credits screen appears, press the joystick button to
start the game.
When the title screen is displayed, move the joystick handle to the right
to get to the preferences screen.

MODIFYING THE PREFERENCES

Move the joystick up and down to select a line.
On a line, move the joystick to the right to make a different option
appear.
Press the joystick button to select the option.

ENTERING YOUR NAME

On the name option, move the joystick down until you reach an empty line.
Then, use the keyboard to type in your name.

OPTIONS

1. Players 1 and 2 can choose between Joystick Port 1 and Joystick Port 2.
   Keyboard 1 (arrows up, down, left, right, and right shift key) and
   keyboard 2 (8, 2, 4, 6, 0 on the numeric keypad).
2. Speed: Full or Half Speed.
3. Number of Players: One or Two.
4. Music: On or Off.
5. Level of Difficulty: 1 or 2.
6. Clear Player Names.
7. CLear High-Score Table.
After selecting your options, press the joystick button to start the game.

TO COMPLETE A LEVEL:

One Player Mode: Collect all Bananas.
Two Player Mode: Collect all Fish and Bananas.
If you lose a life, you automatically return to the last triangle you
touched.
You get bonus lives for collecting treasures, killing enemies, and
completing levels.

MORE OPTIONS

Pressing "p" will pause the game.
Pressing "q" will quit the game.
Have fun!
