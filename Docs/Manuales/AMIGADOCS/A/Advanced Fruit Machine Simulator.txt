PLAYING THE GAME:

MOUSE IN PORT 1....JOYSTICK PORT 2

IMPORTANT: WATCH THE SCROLLING MESSAGE DISPLAY WHEN YOU ARE PLAYING. IT WILL
LEAD YOU THROUGH THE THE GAME AND EXPLAIN WHAT IS HAPPENING.

PRESS 1 TO START GAME,OR PRESS 2,3 OR 4.IF YOU WANT MORE PLAYERS. YOU CAN USE
THE MOUSE,OR JOYSTICK TO MOVE LITTLE ARROW POINTER AROUND TO CONTROL THE GAME,
OR USE THE KEYS SHOWN BELOW.

MOUSE OR JOYSTICK`S CONTROL:

MOVE THE ARROW AROUND THE SCREEN AND PRESS EITHER THE MOUSE BUTTON OR
FIRE(CLICK) WHEN THE ARROW IS POINTING WERE YOU WANT.

INSERTING COINS:

CLICK ON THE COIN PICTURES AT THE TOP OF THE DISPLAY, WHEN YOU`VE
FINISHED.CLICK ON THE `END` ICON TO THE RIGHT OF THE COINS OR ON `START` OR ON
`TAKE`,ALSO  YOU CAN CLICK ON THE `REJECT` OR `GIVE UP` ICONS IF YOU WANT.

DURING THE GAME:

WHEN ONE OF THE FRUIT MACHINE`S BUTTONS IS FLASHING YOU CAN PRESS BY CLICKING
ON IT.

EXTRA INFO:

COINS: YOU CAN SEE HOW MANY OF EACH COIN YOU HAVE AT THE TOP OF THE REELS
SCREEN, AND YOU CAN SEE YOUR TOTAL MONEY-BUT TOKENS DONT COUNT-JUST LIKE REAL
LIFE!

THE GREATEST GAMBLERS:

IN A MULTI PLAYER GAME,ONLY THE WINNER CAN REGISTER HIS NAME AS ONE OF THE
GREATEST GAMBLERS.TO REGISTER, WHEN ASKED TO INSERT COINS,DONT INSERT ANY JUST
END BY PRESSING `START` OR `TAKE` OR CLICKING ON END. THEN WALK AWAY WITH THE
MONEY YOU`VE GOT.

MULTI PLAYER GAME:

THE PLAYERS TAKE IT IN TUEN, EACH TURN YOU CAN PUT IN BETWEEN 4 AND 20 CREDITS.
YOU ARE OUT OF THE GAME WHEN YOU GO BROKE. THE WINNER IS THE LAST ONE LEFT.

TO END YOUR TURN:WHEN ASKED TO INSERT COINS DONT INSERT ANY JUST END BY
PRESSING `START` OR `TAKE` OR CLICKING END.
