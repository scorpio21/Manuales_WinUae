SOUND/CONTROLS: (F2)
--------------------
 SELECT THIS BOX TO DETERMINE SOUND EFFECTS.  YOU CAN TURN THE TITLE TUNE
 ON OR OFF, THE GAME'S SOUND EFFECTS ON OR OFF, OR TOGGLE BETWEEN USING
 THE TITLE TUNE OR THE SOUND EFFECTS THROUGHOUT THE GAME.

OPTIONS: (F6)
-------------------
 THIS SELECTION LETS YOU DETERMINE THE TYPE OF MAP YOU MUST NAVIGATE
 DURING THE GAME.

COMPUTER DATABASE:
-------------------
 USE THE MOUSE TO CLICK ON THE DESIRED INFORMATION CATEGORY
  
   NEAR      NEAREST TARGET
   COM       MOST RECENTLY REPORTED TARGET ON MESSAGE PANEL
   AVE       ALLIED OR ENEMY DATABASE
   CAT       CHANGE CATEGORY

 ON EITHER SIDE OF THE CENTER -MFD- ARE SIX STATUS INDICATOR LIGHTS. CLICK
 ON THE STATUS LIGHT TO CHANGE ANY OF THESE ELEMENTS (OR USE THE CONTROL
 KEYS). 
 
   RL      BARREL ROLL
   W       WEAPON SELECT
