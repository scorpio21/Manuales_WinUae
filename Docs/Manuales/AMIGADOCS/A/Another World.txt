       Ferrari. After Going Underground With The Elevator And Passing 2
       Security Checks , The Professor Opens His Computer To Work On His
       Latest Project : ""PROJECT 23"". It Is A Project About Molecular

       The Professor Disappeared In ... ANOTHER WORLD.
