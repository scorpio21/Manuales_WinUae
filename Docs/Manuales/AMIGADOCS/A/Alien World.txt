THE STORY
---------
A JOINING(WEDDING) CEREMONY IS ABOUT TO TAKE PLACE IN THE VILLAGE OF 
MOGROTH. KAI AND HIS SWEETHEART MEDB ARE ABOUT TO BE PRONOUNCED ONE BY
THE VILLAGES HIGH DRUID WHEN MEDB IS KIDNAPPED BY THE DEMON OF THE COHORTS
OF SLOUGH FEG AND TAKEN TO THE FAR OFF `CAVES OF DREAMS` SLOUGH FEG`S LAIR.

KAI SWORE NO MATTER WHAT, HE WOULD FIND MEDB AND FREE HER FROM THE EVIL
FEG, UPON HEARING HIS OATH, FEG PLACED AN ENCHANTMENT ON KAI, CHANGING HIM
INTO A CREATURE, THE LIKES OF WHICH HAD NEVER BEEN SEEN BY MAN OR BEAST. AN
ALIEN WITH THE SPIRIT OF A HERO.

YOU NOW CONTROL THE MOVEMENTS OF KAI ON HIS QUEST TO REACH MEDB AND FREE
HER. THE JOURNEY IS NOT HOWEVER WITHOUT ITS PERILS.
FEG LET LOOSE HIS DEMONS IN AN EFFORT TO STOP YOU, AND THERE ARE MANY TRAPS
ALONG THE ROUTE, INCLUDING ROCKFALLS-VOLCANIC FIREBALLS AND ACID DROPS.
HOWEVER, YOU ARE NOT UNARMED IN YOUR TASK. BEFORE YOU LEFT, THE DRUIDS
ENDOWED YOU WITH THE POWER OF MAGIC. THIS POWER DOES NOT COME CHEAP.

LONG AGO IN THE TIME BEFORE MEMORY, THE WARRIOR DRUIDS FORSEEN YOUR PERILS
AND HAD PLACED `ORBS OF DESTINY` IN THE CAVES FOR YOU TO GATHER.

`S` SPEED `ORBS` ENABLE YOU TO MOVE FASTER THAN NORMAL
`P` POWER `ORBS` INCREASE THE POWER OF WHATEVER WEAPON YOU ARE CARRYING
`E` ENERGY`ORBS`SEND A SURGE OF RAW ENERGY THROUGH YOUR BODY, REPLENISHING
                YOUR VITAL FORCES, AND CAUSING A SHOCK WAVE THAT WILL
                DESTROY ANY DEMONS ON THE SCREEN. AS YOUR ENERGY RUNS OUT
                YOUR HEART BEGINS TO BEAT FASTER. BUT EVEN THE HEART OF A
                HERO CANNOT STAND THE STRAIN FOREVER.
                
OTHER ORBS CONTAIN WEAPONS FOR YOU TO DISCOVER AND MASTER.

THE WARRIOR DRUIDS LEFT YOU ONE MORE GIFT - A CLOAK ON INVISIBILITY. THAT
WILL SHIELD YOU FROM THE GAZE OF THE DEMONS FOR A FEW SECONDS AS YOU START
OR RESTART EACH LEVEL.

FINALLY AT VARIOUS POINTS AND AT THE END OF THE LEVEL YOU WILL ENCOUNTER
THE MUCH STRONGER GUARDIAN DEMONS, THESE MUST BE SHOT A NUMBER OF TIMES AND
NEED TO BE HIT IN A CERTAIN PLACE. WHEN A HIT IS REGISTERED THE DEMON WILL
FLASH.

AT THE END OF THE GAME YOU WILL ENCOUNTER THE HIDEOUS SHAPE OF SLOUGH FEG
HIMSELF. YOU MUST DESTROY HIM BEFORE YOU CAN ENTER THE METAMORPH CHAMBER TO
REGAIN YOUR NORMAL FORM, THEN YOU WILL BE ABLE TO FREE MEDB AND RETURN TO
THE VILLAGE.

OVERVIEW
--------
AT THE START OF THE GAME YOU WILL BE CATAPULTED INTO THE `CAVES OF DREAMS`
ALL AROUND YOU ARE DEMONS POISED TO ATTACK UNLESS YOU GET THERE FIRST.

KEYS
