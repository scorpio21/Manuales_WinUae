MISSION ELEVATOR
 
  
YOU MUST REACH THE 62nd FLOOR OF THE BUILDING IN ORDER TO DE-FUZE A BOMB. 
THE HOTEL IS SPLIT INTO UNITS OF 8 FLOORS EACH. 
GET THE MASTER KEY, AT START OF GAME, AND SEARCH THE ROOMS FOR THE JANITOR 
TO GET THE EMERGENCY EXIT KEY - ALLOWING YOU TO GET TO THE NEXT 8 FLOORS. 
 
YOU MUST RETRIEVE 16 PARTS OF THE CODE NEEDED TO DEAL WITH THE BOMB - TWO 
PARTS ARE HIDDEN ON EACH SECTION OF 8 FLOORS, AND EACH PART WILL SPECIFY A 
DIRECTION. TAKE CARE TO REMEMBER THE ORDER IN WHICH YOU FIND THEM. DO NOT 
FORGET TO SEARCH FURNITURE ETC. 
 
TO OPEN A DOOR, GO TO THE RIGHT DOORPOST, LOOK LEFT, THEN MOVE THE JOYSTICK 
DIAGONALLY DOWN. 
 
 
SCREEN INFORMATION: 
------------------- 
 
1   SCORE 
2   NUMBER OF LIVES 
3   KEY -  K1   -   YOU ARE IN POSSESION OF THE MASTER KEY 
        -  ED   -   YOU ARE IN POSSESION OF THE EMERGENCY EXIT KEY 
4   INFORMATION SCREEN 
5   TIMER - INDICATES HOW LONG TO COMPLETE YOUR MISSION
