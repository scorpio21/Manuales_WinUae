				MOUSETRAP

TO LOAD:
	Boot Kickstart Version 1.2, this program will also boot from CLI.

	Place disk in Drive 0, the disk has autoboot.

GAME:
	Marvin isn't feeling too happy - his girlfriend Meryl has just left
him for another mouse, saying that she thought Marvin would never amount to
more than an artful cheese scavenger. Marvin, however, has other ideas. In
an effort to win back the affections of his childhood sweetheart, he sets
out on his quest to amass a fortune by doing what Meryl says he does best,
scavenging. So, with your help, his long journey to fame, fortune, and
hopefully Meryl, begins. Marvin's future is in your hands!!!

CONTROLS:

JOYSTICK OR KEYBOARD

	Z		= LEFT
	X		= RIGHT

RIGHT HAND SHIFT KEY	= JUMP
	F		= FREEZE
	G		= TO CONTINUE
	M		= MUSIC ON/OFF
	ESCAPE		= TO END GAME
	Q		= SUICIDE KEY

SPACE BAR TO START GAME
