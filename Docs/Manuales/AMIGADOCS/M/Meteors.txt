The aim of the game is to destroy as many astroids as possible.

For the big astroids you will get 1OO points, for the middle astroids
 2OO points and for the small ones 3OO points.

If you succeed a level, you will get 1OOO extra-points and every 1OOO points
 you will get a extra-life.
