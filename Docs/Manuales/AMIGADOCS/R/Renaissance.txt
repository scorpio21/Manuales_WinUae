ABOUT RENAISSANCE

Renaissance is in two versions, each contains four of your favorite arcade
classics.

The CLASSIC version uses simple graphics and sound effects and emulates the
classics we all know and love.  The CONTEMPORARY versions use modern
graphics and add new features such as extra weapons or shields to bring
these hugely addictive games right up to date.

GAME INSTRUCTIONS

The games are all very simple to play - though very hard to beat!

To select your choice of game, use your joystick to highlight the game and
the type (Classic or Contemporary) you want.

From within each game select how many players you want, using the keyboard.

The on-screen instructions explain the other controls, such as Pause or
Quit.
