                               ROBBEARY

Robbeary is an arcade adventure in which you healp Bertie Bear rob a vast
store. The store has 24 floors and each floor is bristling with guards.
Bertie can climb ladders and jump from one floor to another but he must
avoid being caught by the guards because capture means instant death.

Bertie must collect all the fruit on each level before a key unlocking the
door to the next floor is revealed. At some levels he may come across
another key which opens a door where extra fruit can be collected.

Bertie will come across many objects, some will help him and others he must
avoid.

FRUIT		Collect all the fruit. Each fruit is worth 10 points.

FLOWER		Worth 20 points.

TREASURE CHEST  Can be only opened with a grey color key. Once opened,
                fills the floor with collectable objects.

JEWELS		Worth 100 points

CRYSTAL		Worth 200 points

MAGIC RING	Turns the guards into gems

CANDLESTICK	Turns the guards into diamonds

STOP WATCH	Freezes the guards for a few seconds making them flicker

FUNGI		Freezes Bertie for a few seconds

CROSS		Bertie becomes invincible and can chase the guards turning
                them into edible cup cakes.

GREY CRYSTAL    Doubles the guard speed

GREEN CRYSTAL   Doubles Bertie's speed

GREY KEY        Opens treasure chest

GREEN KEY       Opens the second door for bonus points

GOLD KEY        Opens the door to the next floor immediately

TREAD MARKS     Crumbling platform

DOUBLE ARROWS   Higher jump in the indicated direction

The speed is essential as Bertie has only limited time on each floor

LOADING - Ensure that eh ROBBEARY disk is WRITE PROTECTED. Insert the disk
when the computer asks for WORKBENCH.

CONTROLS - Joystick only, Press Fire Button to jump.
