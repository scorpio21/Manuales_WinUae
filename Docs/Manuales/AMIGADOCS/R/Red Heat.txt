       CONTROLS....  Danko is controlled via the joystick as shown

       JOYSTICK....  Without FIRE being pressed.
                     -------
       LEFT      Danko walks left
     
       RIGHT     Danko walks right

       DOWN      Danko ducks down.

       JOYSTICK....  With FIRE being pressed.
                     ----

       UP         Danko Head butts/punches

       LEFT/UP    Danko High punch    

       LEFT       ..... Shoot/punch

       LEFT/DOWN  ..... Low punch     

       DOWN       ..... Duck down

       RIGHT/UP   ..... High punch

       RIGHT      ..... Shoot/Punch

       RIGHT/DOWN ..... Low punch

       Various bonuses items may be collected and used by pressing fire
       while positioned over them.

       GAMEPLAY.

       As Captain Ivan Danko, Top cop in Moscow`s Homicide Division, you 
       must trace the whereabout`s of Victor Rostavill, known to be head
       of an International Drugs ring.
       The game comprises of 4 levels of cinematic action each increasing
       in difficulty, You begin your struggle in a Russian sauna where only
       skilful hand to hand combat ensures success against a relentless
       string of criminals.
       The action now moves to Chicago where the man from Moscow faces a
       different criminal element. The resulting cultural clash and chilling
       showdown with the drug dealing ~clean heads~ leads to dramatic 
       confronation with Victor himself.
       To complete each section manoevre Danko through the various foes
       you encounter and staying alert to the bonus stages to provide energy
       and firepower you may well need for later stages.

       STATUS AND SCORING

       The main `status screen` displayed between levels, shows current score
       the number of lives remaining, the current stage and difficulty level
       number.
       The main `play screen` shows the amount of bullets remaining at the
       top left(not constantly displayed) and `collection` indicator at the
       top middle.
