JOE BLADE DOCS
--------------

THE STORY
---------

THE YEAR IS 1995, CRIME IS SPREADING OUT OF CONTROL. THE STREETS OF LONDON
ARE CRAWLING WITH EVIL SADISTIC MUGGERS AND CRACK CRAZED PSYCHOPATHIC
PUNKS.

THE PEOPLE ARE TOO SCARED TO GO OUT, AND FEEL TRAPPED IN THEIR HOMES IN
LONDON. THEY ARE LIKE PRISONERS.

ONLY ONE MAN CAN CLEAN UP THE CITY. THAT ONE MAN IS JOE BLADE.
JOE WAS THE SOLE SURVIVOR OF A PLANE CRASH, THE INFANT JOE WAS RESCUED BY
SOME APES. DUE TO THE PRICE OF HUMAN BABY FOOD THEY OFF-LOADED JOE TO
SOME TIBETAN MONKS.
THEY FOUND HIS POWERS AND PUT THEM TO GOOD USE LIKE UNARMED COMBAT, CODE
BREAKING AND UNDERCOVER SURVEILLANCE.

THE MISSION.
^^^ ^^^^^^^^
YOU ARE JOE BLADE ...USING YOUR SKILL OF UNARMED COMBAT YOU MUST CLEAN
UP THE STREETS BY BEATING UP STREET PUNKS AND ANY OTHER RIFF-RAFFS.
IN ORDER TO KILL THEM YOU WILL HAVE TO KICK THEM IN THE HEAD.
WHICH ISN'T DEAD EASY BECUASE THEY LIKE JUMPING TO. NOT ONLY MUST YOU KILL
THEM BUT YOU MUST RESCUE 16 PEOPLE WHO WERE FOOL ENOUGH TO VENTURE OUT
ONTO THE STREETS OF LONDON.


THE SUB GAMES.

SUBGAME 3:

IN THIS SUBGAME THINGS GET MORE COMPLEX.
YOU ARE PRESENTED WITH TWO SETS OF JUMBLED NUMBERS. YOU ONLY HAVE TO
COMPLETE ONE 1, 2, 3, 4, SEQUENCE EITHER TOP OR BOTTOM, BY SWAPPING
NUMBERS BETWEEN THE TWO.
WHEN THE DESIRED PAIR OF NUMBER ICONS (NUMBERS) ARE FLASHING THEN PRESS
FIRE.

SUBGAME 4:

IN SUBGAME FOUR YOU ARE PRESENTED WITH AN EVER CHANGING NUMBER ICON AND FOUR
JUMBLED NUMBERS, THE OBJECT OF THIS SEQUENCE IS TO SWAP THE CHANGING NUMBER
WITH THE FLASHING NUMBER TO FORM THE SEQUENCE 1, 2, 3, 4. WHEN THE DESIRED
PAIR OF NUMBER ICONS ARE FLASHING PRESS FIRE.
