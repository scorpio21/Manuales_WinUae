                   S K Y H I G H   S T U N T M A N


As SUPER STUNTMAN you are hired to shoot another mega action movie by
the top director STEVEN SQUEALBERG.  To get the scenes right you must
complete four death defying stunts each more terrifying than the last!
Each stunt has a RADICAL FINALE, and each presents a different
challenge...but be careful, even this movie can only splash out so
much on your equipment.
If you crash too many times then you're sacked!  Use the POWER UPS to
increase your firepower to PHENOMENAL proportions!

SCENE 1.
Dodge the enemy in a vintage Supermarine Spitfire over war torn
Europe.

SCENE 2.
Armed to the teeth in a full spec military chopper over riot ridden
New York.

SCENE 3.
Flying in a stolen Phantom jet blow the opposition away.

SCENE 4.
Fight the Star War in a laser blasting battle to the death.


HINTS AND TIPS
  -     Memorize the flight paths.
  -     Use smart bombs only when you need them.
  -     Collect all the weaponry that you see.
  -     Destroy the Missile Launchers.

OBJECTIVE OF THE GAME
As the SKYHIGH stuntman you must impress the director of the latest
action movies.  Use all your skill to dodge BULLETS, MISSILES and
LASERS to produce the best action movie yet.

Fly through each level to the end and land your aircraft.  After each
level you will proceed to the next movie.

Collect the extra weaponry that you find on the way.  Use it to wipe
out the on screen bad guys.

Destroy as many of the enemy as you can to make the most of this
movie, but use the smart bombs wisely, there are not many!
