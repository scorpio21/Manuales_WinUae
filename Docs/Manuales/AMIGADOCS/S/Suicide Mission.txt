                     - SUICIDE MISSION INSTRUCTIONS -

Objective : Bomb all the reactors over nine levels. 

                          - Game controls -

From the title screen only: M selects mouse control,
                            J selects joystick control (default),
                            Fire starts the game.

While playing the game:     P to pause,
                            SPACE to restart from pause mode,
                            Q to turn sound off,
                            S to turn sound on,
                            Left button, or Fire button to fire bullet,
                            B to launch a missile (when using joystick),
                            Right Button to launch a missile (mouse)
                            ESC to return to title screen.
                                                  
To turn off the background drumbeat, press P when a new level is
scrolling onto the screen.
You get an extra life every 50000 points.

                               - END -
