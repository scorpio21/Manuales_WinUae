                  SUMMER GAMES I FROM US GOLD : MINI-DOCS

                               GAME LOADING
                               ~~~~~~~~~~~~
Turn On The Computer (Amiga 1000 Owners Must Use Kickstart). When Prompted
For The Workbench Disk, Insert The Summer Games Disk Into The Disk-Drive. The
Game Will Then Auto-Boot. Press FIRE To Quit The Opening Ceremonies, You Will
Then Be Presented With The:

         MAIN MENU (ALL THESE OPTIONS ARE SELECTED WITH THE JOYSTICK)
         ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
1) COMPETE IN ALL THE EVENTS  - Type In The Competitors' Name At The Prompt,
                                Then Select Their Nationality From A Choice
                                Of 18 Countries (A Total Of 8 Players Can
                                Compete). Then You Will Be Asked To Verify
                                The Players' Names. (Up-Yes, Down-No). Press
                                FIRE To Go To The Events Menu.

2) COMPETE IN SOME EVENTS     - See Above!

3) COMPETE IN ONE EVENT       - See Above!

4) PRACTISE ONE EVENT         - Practise your skills in one of the events.

5) NUMBER OF JOYSTICKS        - Choose Number Of Joysticks Used In A Two Or
                                More Player Competition (Select With
                                FIRE-Button).

6) PLAY SUMMER GAMES 2 EVENTS - Merge Summer Games I & II Events Together.

7) OPENING CEREMONIES         - View Opening Ceremonies.

8) CLOSING CEREMONIES         - View Closing Ceremonies.


                      SUMMER GAMES I - EVENTS MENU/INFO
                      ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
A) POLE VAULT       - Firstly Adjust The Bar Height (4.00M-6.20M) With The
                      Joystick (UP/DOWN). Next Choose Your Pole-Grip Position
                      (LOW-HIGH) By Moving The Joystick UP/DOWN. Then Press
                      FIRE To Start Run-Up And Then Pull DOWN On The Joystick
                      When You Are Ready To Vault.

B) PLATFORM DIVING  - You Will Be Presented With A Menu, Where You Can Choose
                      To Attempt One Of The Following: Forward Dive, Backward
                      Dive, Reverse Dive & Inward Dive (Also QUIT).
                      Joystick Controls:
                                                        Un-Tuck
                                                           |
                       FIRE - Begin Dive       Light Tuck--+--Strong Tuck
                                                           |
                                                      Medium Tuck
 
C) 4X400M RELAY     - Press FIRE To Start The Race/Pass The Baton. Push LEFT
                      To Slow Down/Regain Energy, Push RIGHT To Speed Up.

D) 100M DASH        - Waggle The Joystick LEFT-RIGHT To Speed Up.

E) GYMNASTICS       - Press FIRE To Start Run-Up, Then Press FIRE On The Mat
                      To Jump Onto The Trampette. After You Have Jumped Off
                      The Box, Push UP To Tuck And Pull DOWN To Un-Tuck.

F) FREESTYLE RELAY  - Push RIGHT To Dive In, Then Push The Joystick In
                      Whichever Way You Are Facing To Swim In That Direction.
                      When Your First Swimmer Has Completed His Two Lengths,
                      Dive Your Next Man In, And Continue Like This Until The
                      Relay-Race Is Over.

G) 100M FREESTYLE   - See Above!

H) SKEET SHOOTING   - Press FIRE To Launch The Skeets, Then FIRE To Shoot
                      Them. The Usual Joystick Controls Apply To Move The
                      Gun-Sight.


                     

OPTION KEYS:
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Q - Quit Event. 
P - Pause/Un-Pause.
