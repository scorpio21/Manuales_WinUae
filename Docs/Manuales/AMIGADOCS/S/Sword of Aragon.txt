USING THE KEYBOARD AND MOUSE: ALL MENU OPTIONS CAN BE SELECTED EITHER BY
PRESSING THE HIGHLIGHTED FIRST LETTER OF THE COMMAND OR BY CLICKING ON THE
COMMAND WITH THE MOUSE POINTER. TO EXIT A MENU PRESS THE `ESC` KEY OR RIGHT
MOUSE BUTTON.

TO SELECT A UNIT WITH A MOUSE CLICK THE MOUSE POINTER ON THE UNIT(OR STACK OF
UNITS),AND THEN CLICK ON THE UNIT/S TO BE SELECTED. TO SELCT A UNIT WITH THE
KEYBOARD USE THE `MOVE` MENU OPTION. AFTER THEY HAVE BEEN SELECTED,UNITS CAN BE
MOVED OVERLAND, OR IN COMBAT, WITH THE `NUMERIC PAD` OR WITH THE MOUSE.PLACE
THE POINTER IN THE DIRECTION YOU WISH TO MOVE AND THEN CLICK. THE UNITS WILL
MOVE 1 HEX TOWARD THE POINTER POSITION EVERY CLICK.

TO RETREAT A UNIT ONE HEX PRESS THE `ESC` OR THE RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON. TO QUIT
MOVING CLICK THE MOUSE POINTER ON THE UNIT OR PRESS ENTER/RETURN KEY OR THE
`SPACEBAR`.

YOU CAN USE EITHER THE KEYBOARD OR THE MOUSE TO FIRE LONG RANGE ATTACKS(FOR
EXAMPLE BOWS OR SPELLS) DURING COBAT. TO USE THE KEYBOARD, MOVE THE TARGET
CURSOR OVER THE TARGET AND HIT THE RETURN KEY. TO FIRE AT INVISIBLE TARGETS
WITH THE MOUSE CLICK THE MOUSE POINTER ON THE TARGET. IF THE TARGET IS OFF THE
SCREEN. YOU MAY MOVE THE TARGET CURSOR TO IT BY USING THE NUMERIC KEYPAD. YOU
CAN ALSO MOVE THE TARGET CURSOR BY PLACING THE MOUSE POINTER OVER IT, HOLDING
THE BUTTON DOWN AND DRAGGING IT ON TARGET, AND RELEASING THE MOUSE BUTTON TO
FIRE.

WHEN YOU OUTFIT UNITS WITH EQUIPMENT, REMEMBER THAT SOME WEAPONS REQUIRE 2

UNIT THEY COULD NOT USE FOR INSTANCE, HALBERDS OR PIKES..SEE THE EQUIP UNIT

HAND-TO-HAND COMABT:

THERE ARE THREE TYPES OF HAND-TO-HAND ATTACKS: `HAND`, `CHARGE` AND `OVERRUN`.
WHICH OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE TO A UNIT IS DETERMINED BY HOW MANY MOVEMENT POINTS
THEY HAVE AVAILABLE WHEN THEY ENGAGE THE ENEMY. THE `OVERUN` ATTACK WILL
INFLICT THE MOST CASUALTIES TO BOTH SIDE, THE `CHARGE` CAUSES THE NEXT LARGEST
NUMBER, AND THE `HAND` THE LEAST. `OVERRUN` ATTACKS ARE THE MOST EFFECTIVE WHEN
YOU HAVE THE ENEMY GREATLY OUTNUMBERED.

MAGIC SPELLS:

MAGIC SPELLS CAN ONLY BE CAST DURING COMBAT. THIS MEANS THAT THE BRIDGE SPELL
CANNOT BE USED TO CROSS RIVERS DURING OVERLAND MOVEMENT.

EFF----> IS THE EFECTIVENESS OF THE UNIT.THIS IS EITHER THE TOTAL NUMBER OF
SOLDIERS IN REGULAR COMBAT UNITS, OR THE CONDITION OF SINGLE LEADERS.

MV-----> IS THE REMAINING MOVEMENT

THEY MUST RESUPPLY BEFORE THEY CAN SHOOT OR CAST SPELLS. SPELLCASTERS WILL LOSE
ABILITY TO CAST HIGH LEVEL SPELLS WHEN AMMUNITION RUNS LOW, BUT IS NOT YET
ZERO.

UNDER ANY SPECIAL EFFECT.

COMBAT DAMAGE:

MEN AND LEADERS ALL HAVE HIT POINTS, WHICH IS A MEASURE OF HOW MUCH DAMAGE THEY
CAN SUSTAIN. DURING COMBAT THE AMOUNT OF DAMAGE A UNIT SUSTAINS IS DISPLAYEDED
IN TERMS OF HITS, OR THE NUMBER OF HITPOINTS SUBTRACTED FROM THE UNIT. HITPOINT
LOSSES ARE DISTRIBUTED AMONG ALL THE SOLDIERS IN AN INDICATED UNIT OR TO AN
INDICATED LEADER. WHEN ENOUGH HITPOINTS MAY BE KILLED PERMANTLY OR
INCAPACITATED, ASSUMED DEAD, UNTIL AFTER THE BATTLE.
