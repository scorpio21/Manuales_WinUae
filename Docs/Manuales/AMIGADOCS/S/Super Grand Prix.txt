LOADING
-------

A500 - Insert disk and switch on computer.  The title screen should appear
within 10 seconds.


TO PLAY SUPER GRAND PRIX
------------------------

All options and events are selected by "moving and pointing" the racing
drivers hand.

TO MOVE HAND
------------

USE MOUSE or JOYSTICK 2 to move the "glove pointer".


TO POINT HAND
-------------

Press FIRE or LEFT MOUSE BUTTON when over an ICON.  This will SELECT or 
ACTIVATE that ICON.


TO ALTER CONTROLS or NUMBERS OF PLAYERS
---------------------------------------

Move and point the hand on the op half of the title page over the "Super
Grand Prix" logo.  The ICON option selection menu appears.  Now you can
choose:


PLAYERS		-select the number of players 1-4.

CONTROLS	-click on the ICON indicating control for player

VEHICLES	-selct 2, 4 or 6 for the number in the race

LAPS		-select 3, 4 or 5 number of laps for each race

EXIT		-will return to the previous screen

TO SELECT THE EVENT
-------------------

Move the point over one of the 4 pictures showing each event.

FORMULA 1 GRAND PRIX - compete in formula one cars.

.HINTS		-fast and furious, choose a good line and slide round bends


500CC GRAND PRIX-small fast and agile.  

.HINTS		 Avoid to much damage from crashing


DRAG CHALLENGE  -high top speed and acceleration

.HINTS		 turn well before a corner and pull DOWN to use the parachute
 	  	 to slow down before corners.


OPEN CHAMPS.	-a crazy combination of different vehicles

.HINTS		 Find the best overall vehicle, but be prepared for surprises.


Each player selects their VEHICLE in turn by moving and pointing the hand
over the VEHICLE ICON.

N.B. KEYBOARD USERS - Press space to select vehicle.



HOW TO DRIVE
------------

Action		Joystick 1/2	Keyboard	Keypad
Turn left	Left		Z		1
Turn right	Right		X		2
Accelerate	Fire and up	F		3
Brake		Down		C		6

To shoot in the TANK press DOWN.  To launch parachute, do the same.

Press P to pause

Press ESC to quit to results page

Press ESC on results page to quit to menu

DISPLAY PANEL
-------------

This shows damage and no of laps left to complete for each player.  To much
damage will cause your car to EXPLODE!

TO QUALIFY
----------

To qualify to the next race, you must BEAT ALL the computer drivers.  The 
computer cars and bikes are white.
