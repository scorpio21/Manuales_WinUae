Loading Instructions
--------------------

Amiga: Switch on the computer and wait for the disc prompt before 
       inserting the disc.

   ST: Insert the disc and switch on the computer.

   PC: Load MSDOS, insert the disc into drive A and type SOOTY.


Options
-------

Once loaded, press FIRE for the options screen.
Use UP,DOWN and FIRE to select Sooty & Sweep or Snap,
then UP,DOWN and FIRE again to select number of players,
Sooty or Sweep, music or sound and easy or hard.


Keys & Controls
---------------

Amiga/  Joystick 1          Player 1
ST:     Joystick 2          Player 2
        P                   Pause/Resume game
        Ctrl+Esc            Quit to options screen
PC:     Joystick 1          Player 1 or 2
        Joystick 2          Player 2
        F1                  Pause/Resume Game
        F2                  Sound On/Off
        Ctrl+ESC            Quit to options screen
        J                   Recalibrate joystick
        Keyboard            Player 1 only
        Z, Cursor Left      LEFT
        X, Cursor Right     RIGHT
        Q P Cursor Up       UP
        A L Cursor DOWN     DOWN
        Alt Space Enter 5   FIRE


Control in SOOTY & SWEEP game
-----------------------------

LEFT          Walk left
RIGHT         Walk right
UP            Jump
UP & LEFT     Walks up stairs
DOWN & RIGHT  Walk down stairs
DOWN & FIRE   Throw custard pie (after picking up sticky bun)


Controls in SNAP Game
---------------------

FIRE   Stop the reels.
