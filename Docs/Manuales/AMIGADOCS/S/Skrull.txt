             SKRULL THE BARBARIAN
             ====================
                                                TYPED BY SHARD - N&B


No more glimmer!  No more warmth!  Not even the slightest spark!  The world
of  light  will  vanish  for  ever....   Only you, SKRULL THE BARBARIAN the
offspring  of  an  unnatural  love between the SUN WOMAN and SHADOW MAN can
free the Light again, as the Earth collapses in on itself!!  The key to the
Earth`s  salvation  lies hidden somewhere in a labyrinth of dark, damp, and
dangerous caves!!

FEATURES:
---------

QUICK  RESPONSE  ICON  CONTROLS!  TARANTULAS!  VAMPIRE BATS!!  BLOOD FILLED
ROOMS!!!  DRAGONS!!!!  A THOUSAND SURPRISES - NON OF THEM WELCOME!!!

LOADING INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------

ATARI ST

(i)   Insert disk A in the disk drive

(ii)  Switch on your computer

(iii) Click twice on the SKRULL.PRG icon

(iv)  The game will load and start automatically

AMIGA

(i)   Insert the disk in the disk drive DF0:

(ii)  Switch on your computer

(iii) The game will load and start automatically

CONTROLS
--------

MOUSE CONTROL

Use the icon driven controls with your mouse, as indicated below.

To  put  down an object that you are carrying, click first on the icon "PUT
DOWN", then on the object you want to dispose of.

KEYBOARD CONTROL

Use your keyboard, as indicated below.

To  put  down  an  object  that  you  are carrying, press 5 then 1, 2, or 3
depending on the object you want to dispose of.


ACTION                     KEYBOARD                 MOUSE
------                     --------                 -----

STOP                       SPACE BAR                RIGHT CLICK TOP ICON

GO INTO BACKGROUND         I                        LEFT CLICK TOP ICON

GO TO THE RIGHT             --->                    RIGHT CLICK 2nd ICON

GO TO THE LEFT              <---                    LEFT CLICK 2nd ICON

RUN TO THE RIGHT            CTRL --->               RIGHT CLICK 3rd ICON

RUN TO THE LEFT             CTRL<---                LEFT CLICK 3rd ICON

JUMP BACK & SWORD STRIKE    3                       RIGHT CLICK 4th ICON

JUMP FORWARD                I                       LEFT CLICK 4th ICON

SWORD STRIKE No.1           1                       RIGHT CLICK 5th ICON

SWORD STRIKE No.2           2                       LEFT CLICK 5th ICON

TAKE AN OBJECT              4                       CLICK BOTTOM ICON

PUT DOWN OBJECT             5                       CLICK NEXT TO LAST
                                                    ICON BOTTOM ROW
