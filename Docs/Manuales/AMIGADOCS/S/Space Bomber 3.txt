                           SPACE BOMBER 3 - THE FINAL
                              from INTERSOFT
             supplied by: THE CROW - docs written by: THE CROW

Space Bomber 3 is a shoot'em up game, which has the aim to destroy as many
 enemies, as possible.

Futher you have the chance to rescue hostages, who liked to get rescued.

The different enemies(one of them is able to catch & destroy the hostages)
 count a different amount of points.

Some of them will give you, after they got destroyed a extra, which only
 hold for 5 seconds.

The extra's:

L - extra-life
T - dead
P - bonus-point
S - shield for 5 seconds
B - special weapon - a bomb

You could leave the game, by pressing the right/left mouse-button at the
 title-picture.
