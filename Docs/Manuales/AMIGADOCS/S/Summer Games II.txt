                  SUMMER GAMES II FROM US GOLD : MINI-DOCS

                               GAME LOADING
                               ~~~~~~~~~~~~
Turn On The Computer (Amiga 1000 Owners Must Use Kickstart). When Prompted
For The Workbench Disk, Insert The Summer Games Disk Into The Disk-Drive. The
Game Will Then Auto-Boot. Press FIRE To Quit The Opening Ceremonies, You Will
Then Be Presented With The:

         MAIN MENU (ALL THESE OPTIONS ARE SELECTED WITH THE JOYSTICK)
         ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
1) COMPETE IN ALL THE EVENTS  - Type In The Competitors' Name At The Prompt,
                                Then Select Their Nationality From A Choice
                                Of 18 Countries (A Total Of 8 Players Can
                                Compete). Then You Will Be Asked To Verify
                                The Players' Names. (Up-Yes, Down-No). Press
                                FIRE To Go To The Events Menu.

2) COMPETE IN SOME EVENTS     - See Above!

3) COMPETE IN ONE EVENT       - See Above!

4) PRACTISE ONE EVENT         - Practise your skills in one of the events.

5) NUMBER OF JOYSTICKS        - Choose Number Of Joysticks Used In A Two Or
                                More Player Competition (Select With
                                FIRE-Button).

6) PLAY SUMMER GAMES 2 EVENTS - Merge Summer Games I & II Events Together.

7) OPENING CEREMONIES         - View Opening Ceremonies.

8) CLOSING CEREMONIES         - View Closing Ceremonies.


                     SUMMER GAMES II - EVENTS MENU/INFO
                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
I) TRIPLE JUMP      - Press FIRE To Start Run-Up. Then Tap RIGHT To Skip,
                      Then Tap LEFT To Jump.

J) ROWING           - Press FIRE To Start, Then Waggle LEFT-RIGHT To Speed
                      Up.
 
K) JAVELIN          - Tap FIRE Repeatedly To Increase Run-Up Speed, Then Hold
                      LEFT To Begin Throw And Release When You Reach Your
                      Optimum Trajectory Angle.

L) EQUESTRIAN       - Tap UP Repeatedly To Speed Up. Pull DOWN To Turn
                      Around/Trot Back. Press FIRE To Re-Mount Horse (Jumping
                      Is Controlled By The Computer). 

M) HIGH JUMP        - Adjust The Bar Height (1.20M-2.50M) With UP/DOWN. Tap
                      RIGHT Repeatedly To Speed Up. Press FIRE When Ready To
                      Jump.

N) FENCING          - Hold FIRE, And Push LEFT/RIGHT To Move In That
                      Direction. To Lunge, Hold FIRE And Push UP/DOWN.
                      To Control The Sword, Move The Joystick In The Desired
                      Direction Of The Sword, Without FIRE (Eg. UP-Move Sword
                      Up, Etc).

O) CYCLING          - Press FIRE & UP To Start Cycling. Then Rotate The
                      Joystick To Speed Up.

P) KAYAKING         - Joystick Controls:        Paddle Forward
                                                      |
                                   Rotate Canoe Left--+--Rotate Canoe Right       
                                                      |
                                               Paddle Backwards

OPTION KEYS:
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Q - Quit Event. 
P - Pause/Un-Pause.
