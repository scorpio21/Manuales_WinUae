Getting Started AMIGA

Insert Disk 1 in drive, game will Autoboot

The GAME

STRIP-POT is a Fruit Machine simulation which has
five levels, each level has it's own girl, five gorgeous
models in total. The features are listed below.

To start the STRIP POT machine press the space bar.

TRAIL - Numbers on the win line will move you
corresponding number of squares on the trail.

SKULL. Will send you back to the start.

VIDEO. Will play a video strip sequence, pressing the
escape button or the space bar will abort the video.
Floppy disk versions feature Photographs.

DOLLARS. Wins credits

NUDGES. Wins nudges

CREDITS - You start with 50 credits.

NUDGES - When the nudge light flash's you will have 
1 nudge, you may gamble to obtain more nudges. A
nudge allows you to advance the reel one move. Buttons
1,2,3, nudge the corresponding reels.

GAMBLE - Allows you to risk your win, this can be
credits or nudges. This gamble is double or nothing, for
example, gambling on nudges will give you 2-4-8
nudges, you can stop gambling by pressing the
Number 2 key at anytime.

SPIN THE WHEEL

When you get three SPIN THE WHEEL symbols the
wheel is activated, pressing the space bar will stop the
wheel.

The following are the options.

GO UP means that you move up to the next level of
girl

GO DOWN takes you down one level of girl

NUDGES you win nudges

LOOSE you win nothing

CREDITS you win credits

HIT1-COLLECT accepts your win by pressing the 1
key

HIT2-GAMBLE to gamble your winning you must press the 2
key when the flashing light is on win.

HOLD allows you to hold a reel for the next turn
1,2,3, keys are used to hold, you may cancel the hold
by pressing the key again.

WINS AMOUNTS

Three Cherry's: 2 credits
Three Oranges: 4 credits
Three Lemons: 6 credits
Three Plums: 10 credits
Three Strawberry's: 15 credits
Three S: 20 credits
