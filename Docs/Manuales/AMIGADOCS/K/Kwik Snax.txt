THE GAME.
---------

THE GAME IS DIVIDED INTO 4 LEVELS,EACH MORE DEVASTING THAN THE LAST.POINT
DIZZYS GLOVE TO THE REQUIRED LEVEL AND PRESS FIRE.

WEAVE AROUND THE STATIONERY BLOCKS AND PUSH THE SLIDING ONES INTO THE
APPROACHING HENCHMEN TO GET THE FRUIT COMPLETE 5 LEVELS AND FREE A FRIEND.

THERE ARE SPECIAL FEATURES WHICH FLOAT DOWN TO HELP,BUT SOMETIMES THEY WILL
HINDER. A SPECIAL BONUS SCORE IS AWARDED WHEN YOU GET THE FRUIT IN FLASHING
ORDER.

AFTER EVERY LEVEL THERE IS A SPECIAL SLIPPERY SLIDY BONUS SECTION.WHEN DIZZY
STARTS IN A DIRECTION HE IS UNABLE TO STOP HE HITS SOMETHING - ITS THAT
SLIPPERY..PLAN YOUR MOVES WELL - THERE ARE`NT ANY HENCHMEN..BUT YOUR UP AGAINST
THE CLOCK.
