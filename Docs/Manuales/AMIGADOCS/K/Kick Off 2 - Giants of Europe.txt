                 KICK OFF 2 DATA DISK - GIANTS OF EUROPE

  GIANTS OF EUROPE is a data disk consisting of 32 of the best known teams
  in europe.The teams can be loaded in FINAL WHISTLE or RETURN TO EUROPE
  data disk but not in KICK OFF 2 as the name structure is different!

  FINAL WHISTLE:

  SINGLE GAME  :Any two teams can be loaded for a single game.

  1.Select OPTIONS on the main menu.
  2.Select SKILL LEVEL.
  3.INSERT GIANTS OF EUROPE disk in drive a.
  4.Select OTHER SQUAD option.
  5.Team names from giants of europe will be shown.
  6.Highlight the required team and press the fire button.

  LEAGUE OR CUP:

  When the leauge table is displayed,highlight the country name you wish to
  replace. Press f3,to display the GIANTS OF EUROPE directory.

  RETURN TO EUROPE:

  1.Select OPTIONS on the main menu.
  2.Insert GIANTS OF EUROPE disk in drive a
  3.Select league teams option in the options menu.
  4.Select GIANTS file.All 32 teams are loaded as a block.Once the files are
    loaded, remove GIANTS disk and replace it with the appropriate disk!
