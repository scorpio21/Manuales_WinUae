                              MISSION BRIEFING
                              ----------------

TO: Liberator Operative 407
FROM: Mission Control
RE: Liberator Mission No. 342X.1

URGENT - TOP PRIORITY

Proceed at once to planet Hazbal where hostile forces have siezed control
of Planetary Defence System.

IDENTITY OF HOSTILE FORCES - HellRaiser

EQUIPMENT - Standard issue battle-suit, Mk III
       Jimpson Laser Pistol
       Wasp Battle Cruiser

DIRECTIVES - Clear DefSecs 3, 4 and 5 and the surrounding airspace.

EXPECTED RESISTANCE - HellRaiser Battlebots. HellRaiser
    WARNING - Hazbal Planetary Defences will also be operational and must
be destroyed.

                                MISSION DATA
                                ------------

DEFSEC AREAS: To clear installation of hostile forces. To reach ship
necessary to clear airspace.

RESISTANCE: HellRaiser battlebots, HellRaiser scouts, other unknown hostile
fighting machines.

REMEMBER: All installations have teleport systems and can be used to outwit
the HellRaisers.

                                  AIRSPACE
                                  --------
OBJECTIVE: To clear Airspace of HellRaiser Fighters and successfully enter
next defence installation.

RESISTANCE: HellRaiser fighter craft, Planetary defence system.

REMEMBER: You are flying low over the planets surface. Avoid all large
structures. Some of the defence systems is concealed beneath silos; these
can only be destroyed by air to ground weapons.

                               2 PLAYER OPTION
                               ---------------
Mission control have also called upon a fellow liberator. He may assist you
if you require.

If two liberators undertake the mission they must both complete the
immediate task before proceeding further.
