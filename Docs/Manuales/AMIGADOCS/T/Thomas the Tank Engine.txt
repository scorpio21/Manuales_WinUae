+++++++++++++++++ THOMAS THE TANK ENGINE & FRIENDS ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

-+- Full Docs -+-
                                                                         
Control Thomas as he tries to complete all of his tasks before the week is over.

 You must avoid the other trains on the track otherwise you might crash.

 On the normal level you will also have to watch your speed as there are 
objects that have fallen onto the track. You will have to find another way
round them. 

 You may come off the track if you try to go around the corners to quickly.

 Complete each task before the sun goes down or the jobs will be given to 
another engine.

So come on and help Thomas complete all is tasks - it's GREAT FUN !!!
                                                                         
 The game features an easy version for the youngsters and a harder version for 
the game player in the house.
                                                                        
BONUS LEVELS
 Each task contains a secret bonus section. Help Thomas beat his friends in a
race by repeatedly pressing FIRE.
                                                                            
MEMORY GAME
 Play against a friend or the computer. Move the pointer to another card and 
turn that over by pressing FIRE. If they match, you score a point and have 
another go. if they do not match, it is the other player's turn (or the
computer). To win you must get the most matches.
                                                                        
CONTROLS:-

AMIGA/ST/PC:-
 Joystick or keyboard as follows:-
                                 Q  or  P  =  UP
                                 A  or  L  =  DOWN
                                 Z  or  ,  =  LEFT
                                 X  or  .  =  RIGHT
                                 SPACE     =  FIRE

                                 ESCAPE    =  ABORT
                                 F1        =  PAUSE ON/OFF
                                                                           
SELECTING OPTIONS
 Use UP and DOWN then FIRE to select.

SELECTING TASK
 Use LEFT and RIGHT then FIRE to select.
                                                                         
MAIN GAME
 Use LEFT and RIGHT to move Thomas left and right, use UP or DOWN (with LEFT 
or RIGHT) to change tracks. Pressing FIRE makes Thomas move faster.
                                                                           
BONUS STAGE
 Press FIRE repeatedly to move.
                                                                        
MEMORY GAME
 LEFT and RIGHT to move arrow, FIRE to turn over card.
