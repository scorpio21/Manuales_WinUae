AN INTRODUCTION TO THE RACE
---------------------------

Each race consists of two events, the practice which takes place on a 
Saturday, and the final race held on the Sunday.  The practice determines the grid 
position for the race on the Sunday.


HOW TO DRIVE YOUR PORSCHE 944 TURBO
-----------------------------------

Amiga - Joystick only.

Up	-Accelerate
Dowm	-Decelerate
Left	-Steer left
Right	-Steer right
