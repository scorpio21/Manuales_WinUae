:::::::::::::::::::

  SCENARIO

  A fast action simulation of touring Car Racing at it's best.  Practice
  for grid position, enter races and finally compete for a full season
  in your attempt to become Touring Car Champion.

  FEATURES

  High degree of realism including authentic cars and circuits.  Options
  for practise, single race or full season competition.  Hall of fame for
  race wins, Lap records and champions.  High quality graphics.  Heavy
  metal.

  ST/AMIGA Loading:-

  Insert disk into drive and switch on machine.  The program will and
  run.

  To start game from title pages press SPACE (ST) or FIRE (AMIGA).

  Use the joystick to select the type of car you wish to drive, the 
  track on which you want to race, number of laps and whether you 
  want to practice, race or compete for a season (all tracks).

  JOYSTICK Controls:-

      Accelerate
          |
  Left  --O--  Right    FIRE + Accelerate = Change Gear up!
          |                  FIRE + Brake = Change Gear down!     
        Brake

  HIGHSCORE Instructions:-

  At the end of the race if you have qualified for the high score table
  enter your name by typing it in from the the Keyboard and pressing 
  return.
