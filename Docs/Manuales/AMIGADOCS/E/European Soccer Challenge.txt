LOADING INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------

You  are  advised  to  disconnect  all hardware from your computer.  Insert
Kickstart  if  required.   Insert the game disk.  The game will then take a
few seconds to load.

NOTE

Do not remove the game disc while the game is playing.

CONTROLS
--------

N.B.   The  player  that you control is your highlighted player nearest the
ball.

You  must  use a joystick.  Player 1 should plug into Port 2.  Player 2, if
applicable into Port 1.

JOYSTICK CONTROLS
-----------------

UP         MOVE UP PITCH
LEFT       MOVE LEFT 
RIGHT      MOVE RIGHT
DOWN       MOVE DOWN PITCH

Press  FIRE  to  kick  the  ball in the direction that you are facing.  The
longer you press the fire button, the further the ball will travel.

WITH FIRE BUTTON HELD

UP         TACKLE UP PITCH
LEFT       TACKLE LEFT
RIGHT      TACKLE RIGHT
DOWN       TACKLE DOWN PITCH

For  a  throw  in,  or  goal kick, press FIRE in the direction that you are
facing.

To abort the game press F10
