                            E-SWAT CONTROLS
                            ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

  WITHOUT FIRE

          A   B   C
           \  l  /
            \ l /
        H----------D
            / l \
           /  l  \
          G   F   E

  A = JUMP LEFT
  B = AIM UPWARDS
  C = JUMP RIGHT
  D = WALK RIGHT
  E = CRAWL RIGHT
  F = CROUCH
  G = CRAWL LEFT
  H = WALK LEFT

  WITH FIRE BUTTON DEPRESSED

          A   B   C
           \  l  /
            \ l /
         H---------D
            / l \
           /  l  \
          G   F   E

  A = JUMP UP & CLIMB ONTO HIGHER LEVEL
  B = FIRE UPWARDS
  C = JUMP UP & CLIMB ONTO HIGHER LEVEL
  D = FIRE RIGHT
  E = FIRE RIGHT WHILE CRAWLING
  F = JUMP DOWN
  G = FIRE LEFT WHILE CRAWLING
  H = FIRE LEFT

  JOYSTICK IN PORT 1 - PLAYER 2
  JOYSTICK IN PORT 2 - PLAYER 1

  LEFT SHIFT = PLAYER 1 SPECIAL WEAPONS
  RIGHT SHIFT = PLAYER 2 SPECIAL WEAPONS

  P = PAUSE/UNPAUSE
