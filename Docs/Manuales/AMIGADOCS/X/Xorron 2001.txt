			XORRON 2001

We are pleased to present this title as part of our software line for the
Commodore Amiga. We hope you're going to enjoy it.

In order to play Xorron 2001 you require an Amiga with a minimum of 512K of
Ram and color monitor or TV.

INSTRUCTIONS

Set up your Amiga in the usual way. Insert the Xorron 2001 disk when the
Workbench prompt appears.

Press your left mouse or joystick button to exit the introduction demo
mode.

Once Xorron 2001 has completed loading press the joystick button to plunge
you into the realms of Xorron 2001.

The Xorron 2001 disk must be in the internal drive at all times during the
game play.

KEYBOARD CONTROLS

'P'   - Pause On

'ESC' - Start again at the beginning of your current level

'Q'   - Restart game

JOYSTICK CONTROLS

LEFT	- Move left

RIGHT	- Move right

UP	- Climb a ladder or get the elevator

DOWN	- Climb a ladder down or get the elevator down

BUTTON	- Jump or shoot

SCENARIO

Step into the year 2001 AD. All energy resources on the earth are
exhausted. The only hope for new energy are the orbit station Xorron and
you. You are on the planet Zaidon 1 where Xorron is stationed. Search for
energy on Zaidon 1. Collect all and return to the obit station to save the
human race. Your way takes you through 21 amazing levels to find all energy
and another 21 levels to return to your space ship.

Good Luck, you'll need it!

XORRON 2001 PLAYING INSTRUCTIONS

COLLECT PIECES

In case you're in front of a monitor move your joystick up and down and you
collect the contents.  If you find some special-actions, it will be
displayed in the message window on top of your screen.

EXIT A ZONE

You can exit a zone if you have collected all energy and looked in all
monitors. In case you forgot some pieces you have to return to find them.
When all is done on your current level the color of your title bar will
change to a gray color. Staying in front of the exit and moving the
joystick up and down you can enter the next level.

SPECIAL ACTIONS

LASER EYES

Look for a monitor with an orange stone in it to get the Laser Eyes. you
can activate the Laser Eyes with the space bar. A small counter on the
screen will let you know how much energy you have left for your Laser Eyes.
As long as the Laser Eyes are activated you are not able to jump. In case
you come into a situation where you have to jump press the spacebar to
deactivate your Laser Eyes.

LASER EYES INTENSITY PART

This piece gives you more power for your Laser Eyes.

SPECIAL ELEVATOR CARD

Needed to use for some special elevators.

ID CARDS

Pass some doors only with special ID cards.

POISON

Don't touch monitors with a displayed "P".

FREEZE

Collect a monitor with a "ZZ" in it to Freeze your enemies for a while.

STRONG MAN

A small arrow gives you more power to jump long distances.

LIFEPOWER

Get 50% more lifepower by collecting a small cross.

SPECIAL EXIT CARD

You can leave the actual level without collecting all the pieces.

QUESTION MARK

Maybe on all of these special actions.

SPECIAL ROBOTER

Touch a Roboter looking like you for a bonus life. If you shoot him you
will lose your life. Be careful with your green reflected image. If you
touch him you will lose one life. Shoot him to get a bonus life.

ZONE GAMBLER

Press the joystick button to climb high to get a good bonus.  if you are
able to climb up to the top of the Zone Gambler you can start the next game
at your last finished level.
