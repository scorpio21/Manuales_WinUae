The Game..
~~~~~~~~~~
Just as in the film The Penguin is trying to take over Gotham City.Batman
is Gotham's only hope.

Through five levels,from pavement to roof top,you must use your skill and
batweapons to disarm all of The Penguin's evil helpers.They are all here;
The red Triangle Circus Gang,Stilt-walkers.Catwoman and finally,in a 
roof top battle the destructive Penguin himself.
As Batman works his way round the city he will find a number of things to
help him.They can be collected by jumping over the sysbol that represents
the object.

Weapons
~~~~~~~
 Batarang	 - Batmans version of the boomarang.
 Batrope	 - Use this to lasso enemies.
 Sonic Time Bomb - Stuns foes with sound.
 Batdisk	 - Useful for disarming the motor cycle gang.

Pickups
~~~~~~~
 Energy 	 - In the form of a Batsymbols.
 Extra Points	 - x2 or x4 Symbols.
 Batsuits	 - Represented by Bathelments

The Utility Belt
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
This shows your status and is displayed at the bottom of the screen.The
weapons collected are shown on the left of your belt.These are three spaces
for weapons.The weapon you have currently selected for use is shown in the
Batman weakens.Your score is shown above the energy bar.
