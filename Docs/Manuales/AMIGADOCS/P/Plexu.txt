THE STORY

Plexu, the sole survivor of an expedition sent out 20 years ago,
returns to his home planet. During these years an evil robot has
overthrown the king and now rule the kingdom. Your mission is to
defeat this robot and free the planet from his army of evil creatures.
But beware...... at the end of each level resides a large guardian which
must be destroyed to progress to the next level. GOOD LUCK !!!

THE GAME

Collect crystals which can be used as paymentin the shops, and remember
not to run out of time. Red crystals counts as two blue ones (PC).
Large crystals counts as two small ones (AMIGA).

SHOP AND WEAPONS

A shop will appear after you have completed each level. In the shop you
can purchase extra weapons and lives. Use your joystick to select the
items you want to buy. Press fire to buy. (Amiga) Use "up and down" keys
to select and "fire" to buy. (PC keyboard) In the top left corner of the
screen you can see how many crystals you have collected and its price.
SMART BOMB - kills everything on the screen DOUBLE SHOT - kills every-
thing it hits MISSILE - for use against flying monsters SUPER SHOT - !!!

THE CONTROLS

Keyboard control (PC) Default settings: -Cursor keys to move man
  - 'Z' to shoot    - 'Alt' to jump   - 'S' to detonate smart bomb
  - 'I' to activate invulnerability   - 'P' to pause the game
PS!  Keys can also be defined by selecting '2' on the title screen
PSS! Keyboard: Down+Jump to jump down from plateau
Amiga.. On the title screen press left mouse button music on, press
right mouse button music off. During the game press left mouse button
to pause game, press left button to continue the game. Go back to title
screen by pressing right button during pause-mode. Right mouse button
to detonate smart bomb.

LOADING INSTRUCTION

- Switch on the machine and insert disk (Amiga)
- To install Plexu on hard disk C:, make the drive containing Disk One
  of Plexu your current drive, then type 'INSTALLH C:'. If your
  hard disk has a drive letter other than C: , use this letter instead
To play Plexu, type 'PLEXU'. If you have a MCGA graphics adapter ,
  type 'MCGA' .
