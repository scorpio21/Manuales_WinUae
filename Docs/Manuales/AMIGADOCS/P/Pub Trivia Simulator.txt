LOADING THE GAME.

1. Switch on your Amiga.
2. When Workbench is requested, insert game disk.
3. Leave game disk in computer whilst you play the game.


CONTROLS

                      Keyboard           Joystick
Left                  Cursor Left          Left
Right                 Cursor Right         Right
Select/Press Finger   Enter/Space          Fire

Use Joystick Up/Down or cursor keys up/down to select an option from 
the menu.

When you start a game you will have to select a contestant from contestents
row to represent you.  Do this by either pressing 1-5 or using left/right
select controls.

The object of the game is to answer questions correctly in the HOUSE OF 
CARDS and to win money in the MONEY MAZE.  The players take it in turns
to play the game - as soon as player 1 gets a question wrong player 2 plays
etc. etc.  To pick a card from the house of cards use left and right
controls to move your hand and fire/enter to select.  If you answer a
question correctly you proceed up the house of cards or money maze.  Each
player starts with 10 pence and each game costs 10 pence.  If you win
money on the money maze you can use it to continue your game and increase
your score.
