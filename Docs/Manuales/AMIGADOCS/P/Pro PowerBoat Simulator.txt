Pro PowerBoat Simulator
Typed by Belgarath


Loading on Commodore Amiga:

1. Switch on your Amiga.
2. Insert KICKSTART disc if required.
3. When WORKBENCH is requested insert game disc.

The game will now load


Loading on Atari ST:

Switch on the machine and insert the disc.


The aim of the game:

Race to the end of each stage within the
allotted time limit. Keep an eye on your fuel
level, replenish it by collecting the "F"
markers. Gather small static boats for bonus
lives, flags are worth 500 points. Drop mines
to destroy the other boats. Extra mines can
be obtained by running through the mine
markers. Speed over the ramps to leap
anything in your way, the faster you hit the
ramp the further your powerboat will fly.


Controls:

Up          Q
Down        A
Left        [
Right       ]
Drop mine   Space.

The player can also use a joystick.

Press "P" to pause, "ESC" will quit the game.
