Reference Card for Amiga Computers

To copy the Treasure chest onto a hard disk:

The six disks included with your package are labeled with the names
of the programs (Games) contained on that disk.

Boot Workbench V. 1.2 or higher.
You may want to create a "New Drawer" and name it after the game you
are copying to your hard drive.
Insert the disk containing the game you wish to copy to your hard drive.
Double click the disk icon to open the disk window.
Double click on the drawer you wish to open, copy all the files from the
drawer to your hard drive.

You may now play the game from your hard disk by double clicking on the
book icon.

To play the games on floppy disk:

We highly recommend making a backup of your original disks.

The six disks included with your package are labeled with the names
of the programs (Games) contained on that disk.

Insert your backup disk containing the game you wish to play, double click 
the disk icon to open the disk window.

Double click on the drawer you wish to open.

You may now play the game by double clicking on the book icon.

(NOTE: You will want to save your game many times while playing the
Lost Treasures many selections.  We recommend using a separate and
clearly labeled save disk for each game you are playing.)
