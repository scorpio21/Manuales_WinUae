
The controls to the "STOLEN HEART" are as follows
-------------------------------------------------

Joystick forward - will make your ship move up the screen
Joystick down - will make you ship move down the screen
Joystick left - will accelerate your ship to the left
Joystick right - will accelerate your ship to right
Pressing the fire button will fire your corrent weapon

Additional Controls
-------------------
Pressing 'P' will pause the game
Pressing 'SPACE' bar will toggle your score on and off
Pressing 'ESC' will destroy all your ships

It is generally agreed that the use of a joystick with optional outfire
will make your life easier and give the karotides a real headache!. We
would like to point out that the true master of cardiaxx will have NO
use for such deveices.

GAME OBJECTIVE
--------------
Your objective is to blast you way thought the different levels and
deep space zones, finally defeating the Kariote guardians. Listen to
your computer as it warns you of your next threat and informs you of
useful bonuses obtained. some alien squadrons must be totally
destroyed before progression in the level is possible wihle with others
object is merely to survice

CONSOLE DISPLAY
---------------
Along the bottom of the screen are several indicators, from left to
right they are SCORE, LIVES REMAINING, SHIP ENERGY, ALIEN
DIRECTION INDICATOR and THE REMAINING FOR LEVEL COMPLETION.
