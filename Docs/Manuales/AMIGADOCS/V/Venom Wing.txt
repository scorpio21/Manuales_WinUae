INSERT DISK 1 INTO DF0..ON COMPLETION OF A LEVEL,SHOULD A DISK APPEAR ON
SCREEN,INSERT TYHE REQUESTED DISK AND PRESS SPACE BAR TO CONTINUE.

FROM THE TITLE SCREEN,OR THE HIGH SCORE SCREEN YOU CAN BEGIN PLAYING THE GAME
BY PRESSING;

F1; ONE PLAYER MODE
F2; TWO PLAYER MODE
F3; AS A TEAM

CONTROLS;  (SPACE BAR) TO PAUSE.   (ESC) TO QUIT.

SINGLE PLAYER MODE; USE JOYSTICK IN PORT 2
WHEN AS A TEAM ;     JOYSTICK IN PORT 1 FOR CONTROL OF THE POD

SCORE IS DISPLAYED AT THE BOTTOM OF THE SCREEN,ALONG WITH LIVES REMAINING,SHOOT
THE POWER-UP PODS THREE TIMES AND THE SHIP TRANSFORMS INTO AN INVINCIBLE HAWK
FOR A LIMITED PERIOD. HOWEVER,SHOOTING POWER-UP PODS WILL NOT GAIN YOU AN EXTRA
WEAPON.

POWER UP PODS..(LOOK LIKE)

LEVEL ONE    ;--> POWER-UP POD (A CIRCLE WITHIN A CIRCLE)
LEVEL TWO    ;--> POWER-UP POD (A BOX WITH A DIAGONAL BAR)
LEVEL THREE  ;--> POWER-UP POD (A CIRCLE,SURROUNDED BY A CIRCLE)
LEVEL FOUR   ;--> POWER-UP POD (A BROCKEN CIRCLE)

PLAYING TIPS; SHOOT TO KILL, BUT DONT PANIC

LEVEL ONE  ;SHOOT THE ROTATING MISSILES BEFORE THEY EXPLODE INTO SMALLER
            BOMBS, EARN AS MANY EXTRA LIVES AS POSSIBLE FOR THE NEXT LEVEL.

LEVEL TWO  :GO FOR THE EXTRA WEAPONS, AT THE END OF LEVEL GUARDIAN,AIM FOR THE
            EYE, IF ITS A HIT A BLUE FLASH APPEARS.

LEVEL THREE:GO FOR THE SNAKES.

LEVEL FOUR ;DONT LET THE END OF LEVEL GUARDIAN GET TO CLOSE.
