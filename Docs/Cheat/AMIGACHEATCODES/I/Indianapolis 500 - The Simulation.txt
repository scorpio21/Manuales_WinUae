Indianapolis 500


 PLAYING TIPS:

 To take the lap record use the following settings on the Lola/Buick car:

 Fuel............................5 gallons

 Drag............front...........2 notches from top 
                 rear............1 notch from top

 Tyres...........right...........hard
                 left............soft

 Stagger.........................+0.20 inch

 Pressure........right...........24 p.s.i.
                 front left......28 p.s.i.
                 rear left.......25 p.s.i.

 Shocks..........................2 up from firm

 Cambers.........front right....-0.50
                 front left.....+0.25
                 rear right.....+0.25
                 left rear......+1.00

 Gears............................first at 9.30

 To qualify use ten gallons of fuel and first gear at 8.13.
