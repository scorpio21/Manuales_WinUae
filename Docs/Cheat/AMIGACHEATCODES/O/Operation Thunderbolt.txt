Operation Thunderbolt


 CHEAT MODE:

 Get a score of 50,000 points and then let yourself die.
 Enter 'WIGAN NINJA' into the high score table as your name.
 Restart your game, and now [F7] will skip levels.
 Remember that you must save at least one hostage from each of the rescue
 levels if you wish to complete the game.

 Also try typing 'SPECCY MODE' on the high score page for more enimies to
 shoot at.

 Also try entering your name on hiscore table as 'EDOM TAEHC' for infinite
 lives.

 Usually you have to shoot equipment boxes to get the laser sight, but
 press [F8] and fire at the same time on the title screen and you will
 start a 1 player game with laser sight. Press [F2] and fire to start
 a 2 player game with both players having the laser sight.
