Oscar


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the level select screen walk to the Horror level door (screen 2).
 Enter the door as normal and after the level has loaded and before
 you press fire to start the level, push up on the joystick and hold
 down [K]. While still holding both, press the fire button to begin
 the level. Once the level has started you can release the fire button
 and [K] key. Now whenever you want to skip levels just hit [Esc].
