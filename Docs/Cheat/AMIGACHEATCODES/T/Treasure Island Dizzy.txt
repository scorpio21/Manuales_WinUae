Treasure Island Dizzy


 CHEAT MODE:

 During play, pause the game and type in any of these cheats:

      'ICANFLY' - Push up on the joystick to erm, fly.
 'EGGONASPRING' - High jump ability.
   'EGGSONLEGS' - Moving the joystick around in pause mode will change -
                  your current location.

                  Chest - Below the cliff.

      Gravedigger Spade - In the grave on island 2.
            Magic Stone - Totem pole (island 2).
 Detonator and Dynamite - Mine (island 1).
          Axe and Bible - Bridge (island 1).
             Pogo Stick - Pogo place on ship.
             Gold Coins - The shop.
        Fire Proof Suit - Smuggler's cave (island 2).
                 Brandy - Shop.
               Gold Egg - Shop.
              Brass Key - Smuggler's cave.
                Crowbar - Rock in water.

                           SOLUTION (USE TREE HOUSE MAP)
                           -----------------------------
            
      AS A RULE:- DONT HIT ANY WILDLIFE OTHER THAN THE ONES SPECIFIED
      BELOW.PICK UP ANY COINS THAT YOU CAN VISUALLY SEE.SECRET COINS
      WHEREABOUTS WILL BE EXPLAINED BELOW.NEVER GO INTO WATER WITHOUT
      SNORKEL.DONT GO THROUGH FIRE UNLESS YOU HAVE THE FIREPROOF SUIT.
      ALWAYS JUMP THROUGH OR UNDER TRAPS.IN GENERAL CLICK ON THE DOORS
      OF ANY HOUSES NOT MENTIONED ABOVE OR BELOW THAT YOU COME ACROSS.

      KEY:- SAME AS ABOVE + L = LEFT
                            R = RIGHT

      PICK UP CHEST ON THE FIRST SCREEN GO L TO THE BOTTOM OF THE CLIFF.
      DROP CHEST USE IT TO GO UP CLIFF AND L.AS SOON AS YOU ENTER THE NEXT
      SCREEN JUMP L AND HIT THE BEE THUS PRODUCING A SECRET COIN.GO L OVER
      THE BRIDGE.ON THE NEXT SCREEN CLICK ON THE BUSH JUST PAST THE TREE 
      TO REVEAL A SECRET COIN.GO L.GO L UNDER H1 AND KEEP GOING L UNTIL
      YOU GET TO A COIN.GO R BACK TO H1.JUMP UP AND CLICK ON THE DOOR OF
      H1 TO REVEAL A SECRET COIN.GO L & L AGAIN.JUMP THROUGH TRAP ONTO THE
      ABOVE LEVEL AND GO L , L.JUST AS YOU ARE ABOUT TO GO OFF THE SCREEN 
      JUMP LEFT.JUMP LEFT AGAIN THROUGH THE TRAP AND GET THE COIN & JUMP 
      RIGHT THEN RIGHT AGAIN.GO R AND THEN JUMP LEFT.CLICK ON THE DOOR OF 
      H3 TO REVEAL A SECRET COIN.GO UP AND PICK UP THE HEAVY WEIGHT.GO BACK
      TO THE CHAIN AND DROP THE WEIGHT.THIS WILL NOW LOWER THE FLOOR ABOVE
      SO IT IS ACCESABLE.GO UP TO H6 AND PICK UP THE DETANATOR.GO R TO H7
      AND GET THE SPADE.JUMP R OFF THE END OF H7 AND YOU WILL LAND ON THE
      CLOUDS.YOU WILL BE ABLE TO SEE THE POGO STICK SO GRADUALLY WORK YOUR
      WAY UP THE CLOUDS AND PICK IT UP.DROP OFF THE CLOUDS.GO TO H1 AND
      DROP ALL BUT THE DETANATOR.GO ALL THE WAY UP TO H4 AND PICK UP THE 
      SNORKEL.JUMP L OFF THE END OF H4.YOU WILL NOW BE AT THE MINE.PICK UP
      THE COIN AND GO DOWN.GO L UNTIL THERE IS A STONE BLOCKING YOUR WAY 
      AND DROP THE DETANATOR HERE.GO R AND PICK UP THE MAGIC STONE.GO R
      AND UNDERNEATH THE PLATFORM.ON THE NEXT SCREEN CLICK ON THE FIRST
      FLOWER THAT YOU SEE TO REVEAL A SECRET COIN.GO R UNTIL YOU REACH
      H1.DROP THE STONE AND PICK UP THE SPADE AND POGO STICK.NOW GO R
      UNTIL YOU GET BACK TO THE BEACH AND THE FIRST SCREEN.DROP THE SPADE
      PUT THE OBJECTS IN THE ORDER POGO,SNORKEL,SPACE AND GO R INTO THE SEA.
      PATIENCE IS REQUIRED HERE TO WAIT FOR THE FISH ETC TO MOVE 
      SUFFICIENTLY FOR YOU TO GO.GO R AND DOMW PASSING UNDER THE JELLY
      FISH.YOU WILL NOW BE AT THE SHIPWRECK.STAND NEXT TO THE BIG STONE AND
      JUMP LEFT.AND THE UP AGAIN ONTO THE TOP OF THE SHIP.GO R AND JUMP OVER
      THE CRAB.PICK UP THE SALTWATER CROWBAR AND JUMP STRAIGHT UPWARDS.YOU 
      NOW ON `THE ISLAND IN THE SKY` DROP THE POGO STICK AND PICK UP THE
      FIREPROOF SUIT.ARRANGE THE OBJECTS IN THE ORDER CROWBAR,SUIT,SNORKEL.
      WALK OFF TO THE LEFT OF THE ISLAND.GET THE COIN AND GO L AGAIN.YOU
      ARE NOW BACK ON THE SHIP.GO DOWN AND JUMP OVER THE BIG STONE AND GO
      R JUMP R AGAIN UNDER THE JELLY FISH.DROP THE CROWBAR ON TO THE BIG
      STONE AND STAND ON A BUBBLE.ONCE YOU ARE ON JUMP L SO THAT YOU HIT 
      THE BIG GREEN FISH THAT IS SWIMMING.YOU WILL NOW BE INSIDE IT SO
      PICK UP THE COIN AND GO L.GET ON THE BUBBLE AGAIN AND GO R ACROSS
      THE STONE.GO RIGHT AGAIN AND JUMP R.JUMP R AGAIN AND CLICK ON THE 
      BUSH TO REVEAL A HIDDEN COIN.WAIT NOW UNTIL THE SCHOOL OF FISH HAS
      GONE OFF SCREEN (DO THIS EVERY TIME YOU GO THAT WAY SO YOU DONT GET
      KILLED BY THE FISH NEXT TIME).GO R.DROP THE SUIT ON THE STONES NEXT
      TO THE GRAVESTONE THAT SEEM TO BE IN MID-AIR.G R UNTIL YOU GET TO
      THE BIBLE AND PICK IT UP.GO R AGAIN UNTIL YOU GET TO THE PIER AND 
      GET THE KEY.GO L BACK TO THE GRAVESTONE.DROP THE KEY BUT DONT PICK UP
      THE SUIT.GET THE AXE.NOW GOALL THE WAY BACK TO THE ISLAND.
      ARRANGE THE OBJECTS BIBLE,AXE,SNORKEL AND GO LEFT TO THE BRIDGE.
      DROP THE BIBLE ON THE CENTRE OF THE BRIDGE.GO L OFF THE CENTRE AND 
      ARRANGE THE OBJECTS.AXE,SPACE,SNORKEL.GO BACK TO THE CENTRE OF THE 
      BRIDGE AND DROP THE AXE.GO L AND USE THE PLATFORMS TO ALLOW YOU TO
      GET THE TREASURE.GO R AND R AGAIN AND MAKE YOUR WAY UP TO THE TOP
      OF THE SCREEN.WHEN YOU ARE ON THE VERY TOP PLATFORM JUMP UP AND ONTO
      DRY LAND AGAIN.GO R AND OVER THE HOLE IN THE BRIDGE AND DROP 
      EVERYTHING.NOW GO L TO H1 AND PICK UP THE STONE.NOW GO L BUT WAIT 
      UNTIL THE HIGHEST BEE IS AT THE LEFT OF THE SCREEN BEFORE YOU EXIT.
      MAKE YOUR WAY UP TO H2.ONCE THERE GO R AND JUMP TO THE HIGHEST 
      PLATFORM.NOW JUMP R OFF THE END OF THIS AND YOU SHOULD HIT THE BEE
      AND PRODUCE ANOTHER SECRET COIN.GO L AND PICK THIS UP.GO R TO THE
      BRIDGE.JUMP OVER THE HOLE AND PICK UP THE TREASURE AND THE SNORKEL.
      GO R BACK TO THE BEACH.GET SPADE.GO R AND UP ETC ACROSS TO THE OTHER 
      ISLAND.GO R TO THE TOTEM POLE.DROP THE STONE AT THE BOTTOM.ONCE ON 
      TOP KEEP JUMPING R AND PICK UP THE FLIPPERS.WALK OFF R.GO R UNTIL
      YOU SEE THE MAN AT THE STORE.STAND IN FRONT OF HIM AND DROP THE
      TREASURE.HE WILL GIVE YOU A BOAT.GO R TO THE PIER AND DROP THE BOAT.
      GO L TO THE GRAVESTONE.DROP FLIPPERS.PICK UP KEY.ARRANGE THE OBJECTS
      SPADE,SNORKEL,KEY AND DROP THE SPADE WHERE THE SUIT IS.
      YOU ARE NOW IN THE SMUGGLERS CAVE.DROP SNORKEL.GO R JUMPING OVER
      THE BARRELS AS YOU GO.DROP THE KEY ON THE TRAP DOOR.GO DOWN AND 
      GET BRANDY.JUMP BACK UP AGAIN AND DROP THE BRANDY.GO R OVER THE
      BARRELS AND THROUGH THE FIRE.GO R AGAIN UNTIL YOU CAN SEE THE GOLDEN 
      EGG.AS SOON AS YOU GET THE EGG GO L AND JUST AS YOU ARE ABOUT TO GO
      OFF THE SCREEN JUMP L.PICK UP BRANDY AND GO L.DROP FIREPROOF SUIT.
      GO L AND GET SNORKEL.GO L AGAIN BUT LEAVE THE DINAMITE.WALK SLOWLY
      L BEHIND THE FISH AND LEAVE THE SCREEN WHEN IT HAS GONE.GET ON A
      BUBBLE AND GO R ONTO THE STONE.GO R.JUMP OVER THE HOLE WHERE THE
      GRAVESTONE IS AND GO R TO THE STOREKEEPER AGAIN.GIVE HIM THE EGG
      AND THE BRANDY.TAKE THE MOTOR AND PETROL AND DROP THEM AT THE PIER.
      GO L BACK TO THE GRAVESTONE AND GET FLIPPERS.DROP DOWN THE HOLE.
      GO L AND PICK UP THE DINAMITE.USE THE SAME PROCEDURE AS BEFORE TO
      EXIT THE SCREEN ON THE LEFT.DROP DOWN THE HOLE THAT THE BUBBLES ARE
      COMING FROM AND MAKE YOUR WAY DOWNWARD.PICK UP THE COIN AT THE
      BOTTOM AND SWIM BACK UP TO THE TOP AGAIN.ONCE ON THE STONES AGAIN
      DROP THE FLIPPERS.YOU NOW WANT TO GO ALL THE WAY BACK L TO THE
      MINE ON THE OTHER ISLAND.ONCE THERE PICK UP THE DETANATOR AND DROP
      THE DINAMITE NEXT TO THE PILE OF RUBBLE.GO BEHIND THE BIG STONE AND
      DROP THE DETANATOR.ONCE THE STONES ARE GONE PICK UP THE BAG OF COINS.
      NOW GO ON ANOTHER LONG JOURNEY BACK TO THE R ISLAND TO SEE THE 
      STOREKEEPER.GIVE HIM THE BAG OF COINS.TAKE THE IGNITION KEY TO THE
      PIER AND DROP IT.THE BOAT WILL NOW START TO MOVE.AT THIS POINT YOU 
      SHOULD HAVE 29 COINS AND IF YOU HAVENT GO BACK AND EXPLORE EVERYWHERE
      MAKING SURE THAT YOU HAVE CLICKED ON THE DOORS OF ANY HOUSES THAT YOU
      SEE AS WELL AS THE SECRET COINS EXPLAINED IN THIS PASSAGE.
      ONCE YOU HAVE GOT 29 COINS JUMP ON THE BOAT AND WHEN IT GETS FAR 
      ENOUGH JUMP OFF R ON TO THE OTHER PIER.PICK UP THE FINAL COIN AND
      CONGRATULATIONS YOU HAVE COMPLETED TREASURE ISLAND DIZZY.
