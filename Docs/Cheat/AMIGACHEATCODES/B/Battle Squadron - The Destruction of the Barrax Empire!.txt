BATTLE SQUADRON
---------------

CHEAT MODE:

 During play type 'CASTOR' and the screen will flash green.
 This turns the collision detection off and by pressing [F6] to [F10]
 selects the various weapons and [F1] to [F5] will alter it's power.


 EDITOR:

 To activate the game options editor, type 'ELECTRONIC' on the intro
 screen. Use the function keys to operate.
