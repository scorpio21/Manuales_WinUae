 CHEAT MODE:

 To select the league you want to play in, press [L] followed by a
 number from [1] to [3], with 1 being the highest. Now press [M]
 followed by a number from [1] to [6], which will select the match
 you want to play. Therefore if you press [L] [1], [M] [6] this
 will whisk you to the last match of the top league.
 You can now also press any of the following keys during play:

 [L] - Lightening.
 [I] - Invincibility.
 [G] - Walking ball.
 [R] - Sheild.
 [S] - Hare.
 [D] - Tortoise.
 [F] - Freeze blocks.
