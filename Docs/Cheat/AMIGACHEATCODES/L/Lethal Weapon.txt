 CHEAT MODE:

 Whilst playing, press and hold down the [Alt] and [Y] keys and
 any one of the following at the same time for various effects:

   [L] - Increase lives.
   [K] - Increase ammunition.
   [F] - Fly mode.
   [D] - Die.
   [I] - Flash light.
   [Z] - Remove game sprites.
   [M] - Remove game sprites and platforms.
   [N] - Return game to normal.
  [F1] - Mission 1-1.
  [F2] - Mission 1-2.
  [F3] - Mission 1-3.
  [F4] - Mission 2-1.
  [F5] - Mission 2-2.
  [F6] - Mission 3-1.
  [F7] - Mission 3-2.
  [F8] - Mission 4-1.
  [F9] - Mission 4-2.
 [F10] - Mission 4-3.


 TIPS:

 Just before you enter the door to mission 1,  jump on the files that
 are on the floor. Now jump up on to the notice board (far right) and
 press up on the joystick to enter a hidden door. You will now appear
 in a secret room with a devil that is guarding 4 extra hearts/lives.
