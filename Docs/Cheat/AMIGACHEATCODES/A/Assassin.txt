Assassin


 CHEAT MODE:

 On mission one, run right until you come to the first tree.
 Climb up the left side of the tree until you can go no further.
 Now hold down the left [Shift] key and type 'NICEVIEWFROMUPHEREMATE'.
 The screen will flash indicating that the cheat mode is active.
 During this process the game automaticly pauses it self so press [P]
 to unpause. You are now free to press any of the following keys:

        [C] - Continue.
        [E] - Skip to end of level.
        [S] - Extra time.
        [W] - Extra weaponry.
 [1] to [6] - Level select.
