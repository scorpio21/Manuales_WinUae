Assassin: Special Edition


 CHEAT MODE:

 During play simply type 'ANOTHERCHEATMODE', the screen will flash to
 acknowledge the cheat mode has been activated. This gives you infinite
 lives and you can also press any of these keys during play:

        [E] - Skip to end of level.
        [D] - Die.
        [W] - Full weaponry.
     [Help] - Next level.
 [1] to [6] - Tops up weapons.
