Helter Skelter

During play, type "NAMRON".  You should now appear on the next
level and will have access to the following keys:

 '-' (on the keypad)     Skip to previous level
 'ENTER' (on the keypad) Skip to next level

In a two player game, player 1 needs to get an extra life.  Then both
players must lose all their lives.  Player two will get 99 lives, and
after he loses one, so will player one.

To enter the password press 'F1' or 'F2' (one or to players) select
control by pressing fire on the joystick and then you will be asked
for a password.

                            (LEVEL CODES)

      21-"FLIP"       41-"GOAL"       61-"TWIN"

Press 'F10' on the title screen to access the level editor.
