SAINT DRAGON
------------
Whilst the game is playing type the following, followed by a number from 5
to 10!  Remember to push the CAPS LOCK key as well.
LEVEL    Takes you to the next level.
LIVES    Infinite lives.
WEAPON   Gives you a better weapon.
CREDITS  Infinite credits.
