Seven Gates Of Jambala, The


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the title screen, type 'YICKE YACKE H[HNERKACKE' ('ZICKE ZACKE HÜNHERKACKE' 
 on a German   keyboard), hold down the left [Shift] key, hold down [1], release [1], 
 release the left [Shift] key.
 
 The title should turn to blue. During play keys [1] to [7] take you to
 the various levels, [F1] to [F5] selects weapons and [G] gives you gold.
 Check out the other function keys as well.
