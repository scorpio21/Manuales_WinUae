Clown 'O' Mania


 CHEAT MODE:

 On the title screen, press and hold 'G', FIRE BUTTON, LEFT MOUSE,
 and RIGHT MOUSE.  Now use the following keys during play:

               F1      Turn screen yellow.
               F2      ?
               F3      For 255 of everything.
               F4      Speeds up the clown.
               F5      Speeds up the enemies.
               F6      Flips screen upside down.
               F7      Flips screen back.
               L       Skips levels.
