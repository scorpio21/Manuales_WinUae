                                  DIGGERS
                                  -------

START THE GAME AND CHOOSE THE CHARACTER YOU WANT TO BE. ON THE ZONE SELECTION
SCREEN (THE BIG MAP) MOVE THE POINTER OVER THE WORD 'FUJALE' AND PRESS THE
RED BUTTON ONCE. THEN DO THE SAME ON 'CHONSKEE' AND 'DEENA'. YOU SHOULD HEAR
THE SOUND OF A JEWEL BEING FOUND. YOU CAN NOW MOVE YO ANY LOCATION ON THE MAP
INCLUDING THE QUESTION MARKS IN THE CORNER.
