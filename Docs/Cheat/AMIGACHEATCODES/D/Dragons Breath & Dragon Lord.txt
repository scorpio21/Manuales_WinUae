DRAGONS BREATH
--------------

3 RASGON (GRIND)(FULLHEAT) 
3 HALORUS (CUT)(FULL CONDENSER) 
3 IGELE (NORMAL)(FULLHEAT)(MED COND) 
2 RASGON (GRIND)(FULL HEAT) 

GIVES YOU A DRAGON SPELL.

SPELLS: 

(EYESIGHT) 2X RASGON,2X IGELE 1X ACRUS 
(SPEED) 2X RASGON 2X AROLIG 1X ACRUS 
(WISDOM) 2X RASGON 2X HALORUS 1X ACRUS 
(STRENGTH) 2X RASCON 2X ULIN 1X ACRUS 

TRY DIFFERENT WAYS OF PREPARING THESE SPELLS.
