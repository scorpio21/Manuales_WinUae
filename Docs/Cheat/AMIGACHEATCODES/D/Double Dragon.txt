DOUBLE DRAGON
-------------


CHEAT MODE:

 Play the two player game and get both players killed until you have
 one credit left and the CONTINUE message is displayed on both sides of
 the screen. Press both fire buttons simultaneously for infinites. 

 If that doesn't work try the following

 On the title screen, type 'R U CALLING ME PINT A POFF!' and then press
 the [Return] key. You'll now be able to kill any opponent by pressing
 the [Del] key.

 Also try typing in any of these on the title screen or into the high
 score table:

             'LEVEL ONE PLEASE' - Start on level one.
             'LEVEL TWO PLEASE' - Start on level two.
           'LEVEL THREE PLEASE' - Start on level three.
            'LEVEL FOUR PLEASE' - Start on level four.
            'LAST LEVEL PLEASE' - Start on last level.
         'I DO NOT WANT TO DIE' - Invicibility.
   'GIVE ME MORE HEALTH PLEASE' - Lots of energy.
 'GIVE ME LOADS OF TIME PLEASE' - Lots of time.
                 'NEIL HARDING' - For something funny?
