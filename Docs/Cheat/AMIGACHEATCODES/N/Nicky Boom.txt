 CHEAT MODE:

 Entering 'TRONIX' as a password activates these keys during play:

  [F1] - Gives you a shield.
  [F2] - Gives you ten bombs.
  [F3] - Gives you ten keys.
  [F4] - Gives you ten mega bombs.
 [Del] - Skips to next level.
