 CHEAT MODE:

 Hold down any of the following key combinations during play for
 various effects:

               [Ctrl] and [B] - Background scroll stops.
 Left + right [Shift] and [T] - Stops timer.
   Left + right [Alt] and [H] - Collects all hearts.
 Left + right [Amiga] and [S] - Skips level.
